## Part 1 — The 60-Second Window

78% of buyers go with the first agent who calls them back. The first 60
seconds after a Facebook or YouTube form-fill is where deals are decided.
Yet the median ad-lead response time across the industry is over 47
minutes — by which point your prospect has already replied to two other
agents.

### The Speed-to-Lead Equation

`Closing Rate = (Speed × Persistence × Personalization) × Quality of Lead`

The teams winning right now are not the ones with the biggest ad budgets.
They are the ones who answer the phone — or have an AI answer it — in
under 60 seconds, every time, 24/7.

## Part 2 — AI vs. Human ISA Cost Breakdown

A typical inside-sales agent costs $4,500–$7,500/month all-in (base,
commission, payroll tax, training, turnover). They work 40–50 hours a
week, miss every nights-and-weekends lead, and burn out within 18 months.

An AI inside-sales agent costs a fraction of that, works 168 hours a week,
and never has a bad day. The math at $20M+ in production is not close.

## Part 3 — The Facebook & YouTube Lead Playbook

The follow-up sequence top brokerages use to convert ad leads at 6–9%:

1. **0–60 seconds**: AI voice call + SMS, qualifying intent and timeline.
2. **5 minutes**: Personalized follow-up text referencing the ad they
   clicked.
3. **1 hour**: Second outbound call if no answer.
4. **Day 1**: Email with the specific listings or market data they
   requested.
5. **Day 2–14**: Drip of value-first content keyed to their stated
   timeline.
6. **Day 30, 60, 90**: Quarterly long-cycle nurture for "not now" leads.

## Part 4 — AI Response Benchmarks

What sub-60-second response looks like at scale, and the operational
checklist to get there:

- Webhook integration from Facebook Lead Ads → CRM → AI agent
- Pre-warmed phone numbers with proper STIR/SHAKEN attestation
- Voice agent trained on your brand, market, and qualification criteria
- Live handoff path to a human agent for hot leads
- Recording and transcription for every conversation
