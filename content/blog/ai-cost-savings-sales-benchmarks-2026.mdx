## FAQ

**Q: Can AI really replace a human SDR?**
A: AI replaces the *tasks* a human SDR performs — prospecting, outreach, follow-up, qualification, and meeting booking. It doesn't replace relationship building, complex deal negotiation, or strategic account management. The optimal model is AI for top-of-funnel volume + humans for closing qualified opportunities.

**Q: What's the catch?**
A: AI sales agents need proper configuration — ICP definition, messaging templates, CRM integration, and ongoing optimization. Done-for-you platforms handle this; DIY platforms require technical resources. The "catch" is that lazy setup produces lazy results.

**Q: How do I measure if it's actually working?**
A: Track cost per lead engaged, cost per meeting booked, cost per SQL, and response time to new leads. Compare these metrics before and after AI deployment. Most businesses see measurable improvement within 30 days.

**Q: Is this only for outbound sales?**
A: No. AI lead response (inbound) typically produces faster ROI than AI outbound because inbound leads have higher intent. The biggest revenue recovery comes from answering inbound leads in under 60 seconds vs the industry average of 42 hours.
