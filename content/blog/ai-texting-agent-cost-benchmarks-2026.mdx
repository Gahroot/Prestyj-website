## Three Pricing Models: What You're Actually Buying

### Model 1: Self-Serve Platform (DIY)

You sign up, configure the AI, connect your CRM, and manage compliance yourself.

| Cost Component | Monthly Range |
| --- | --- |
| Platform subscription | $200 – $800 |
| Per-message overages | $0.02 – $0.08/msg |
| 10DLC, phone numbers, carrier fees | $18 – $85 |
| CRM integration | $0 – $100 |
| **Total monthly** | **$220 – $1,000** |

**Hidden cost:** Time. Average setup is **8–15 hours** the first month, with **2–5 hours/month** ongoing. At $75–$150/hour for owner time, that's **$600–$2,250 in implicit cost** that never shows on the invoice.

### Model 2: API-Based / Usage-Based

Pay per message with no monthly platform fee. Requires developer resources.

| Volume | At $0.03/msg (all-in) | At $0.08/msg (with AI processing) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 500 messages | $15 | $40 |
| 2,000 messages | $60 | $160 |
| 5,000 messages | $150 | $400 |
| 10,000 messages | $300 | $800 |

**The catch:** Building your own conversation management, CRM integration, and compliance handling costs **$5,000–$25,000 in initial development** plus ongoing maintenance.

### Model 3: Done-for-You Managed Texting

Full-service provider handles strategy, setup, compliance, and ongoing optimization.

| Cost Component | Monthly Range |
| --- | --- |
| Managed service fee | $500 – $2,000 |
| Platform, compliance, strategy | Included |
| **Total monthly** | **$500 – $2,000** |

**The real cost advantage:** For owner-operators earning $150/hour equivalent, self-serve platforms actually cost **$950–$2,600/month** when you add the time investment ($750–$1,800/month). Done-for-you at $500–$2,000/month with zero time investment is cheaper for most service businesses.

## Hidden Costs Nobody Warns You About

| Hidden Cost | Monthly Range | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Carrier surcharges** (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile) | $30 – $115 | Per-message fees passed through at 5,000 msgs/mo |
| **10DLC registration & maintenance** | $5 – $20 | Brand + campaign registration, monthly upkeep |
| **Phone number rental** | $3 – $15 | 1–3 local or toll-free numbers |
| **Opt-out & TCPA compliance** | $30 – $100 | If not bundled with your platform |
| **CRM integration** | $0 – $100 | Native connectors free; Zapier or custom costs extra |
| **Overage charges** | 30–60% premium | On messages exceeding your plan allowance |

**Budget an extra 15–30%** on top of your platform fee for true monthly cost. Any vendor quoting a flat number without disclosing these components is either absorbing them (good) or hoping you won't notice (bad).

## How to Calculate Your AI Texting Budget

**Step 1:** Count your monthly leads + missed calls + dormant contacts.
**Step 2:** Estimate message volume (leads × 8 msgs/conversation + 25% buffer).
**Step 3:** Add 15–30% for hidden costs (carrier, compliance, numbers).
**Step 4:** Project revenue impact (improvement in conversion × average job value).
**Step 5:** Calculate break-even (total cost ÷ average job value = bookings needed). Most businesses break even at **1–3 additional bookings/month**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does an AI texting agent cost per month?
**$200–$2,000/month** for platform plans, with done-for-you starting at $500/month. Average fully-loaded cost: **$340/month** (small business) to **$1,450/month** (mid-market). See our [AI Texting Agent Pricing Guide](/blog/ai-texting-agent-pricing-guide).

### What is the cost per message for AI texting?
**$0.02–$0.15 per message** depending on platform type and volume. Carrier surcharges add $0.003–$0.005. The key metric is [cost per booked appointment](/stat/lead-response-ai-cost-per-lead-engaged) ($2–$12), not cost per message.

### Is AI texting more expensive than manual human follow-up?
No — AI texting is both cheaper and higher performing. Manual follow-up costs **$2.65–$6.75 per contact** total; AI costs **$1.62–$5.55 per contact** while responding 120–240x faster and converting 2–3x more leads. See [AI Texting Agent vs Human SDR](/blog/ai-texting-agent-vs-human-sdr-2026).

### What hidden costs should I budget for?
Add **15–30%** for carrier surcharges ($30–$115/mo), 10DLC compliance ($5–$20/mo), phone numbers ($3–$15/mo), and opt-out management ($30–$100/mo if separate). Read [hidden costs of AI lead response systems](/blog/hidden-costs-of-ai-lead-response-systems-2026).

### How long to see ROI?
Most businesses see **measurable ROI within 30–60 days**. Missed call text-back produces immediate recovery. Reactivation campaigns show results in 60–90 days. Breakeven at 1–3 extra bookings/month.

### Per-message or monthly subscription?
For **2,000+ messages/month**, flat-rate subscriptions almost always win. Per-message makes sense only under 500 messages/month. See [Best AI Texting Agent for Service Businesses](/blog/best-ai-texting-agent-for-service-businesses-2026).
