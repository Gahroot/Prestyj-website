## Hidden Costs Insurance Buyers Miss

### 1. Per-Display Micro-Fees
Some providers charge $0.005–$0.02 every time your brand displays on a recipient's screen. At 10,000 dials/month with 90% display rate, that's 9,000 displays × $0.01 = $90/month extra.

### 2. Attestation Setup Fees
One-time $0–$500 for STIR/SHAKEN attestation. Amortize over 12 months: $0–$42/month.

### 3. Number Porting and Re-Attestation
If you change carriers or re-issue DIDs, you may need to re-attest numbers ($0–$100 per number).

### 4. Contract Lock-In
Annual contracts prevent you from switching if coverage degrades or pricing changes. Month-to-month costs 10–20% more but provides flexibility.

### 5. Logo/Name Approval Delays
5–15 business days for branded name and logo approval. Factor this into pilot timelines.

## Insurance-Specific Pricing Considerations

### Renewal Campaigns (Seasonal Spikes)
Insurance agencies see call volume spikes during renewal seasons (auto: 6–8 weeks before policy expiration; home: 30–60 days before). Some providers charge overage fees for volume spikes. Ask about:

- Overage pricing above your contracted line count
- Ability to add temporary lines during peak seasons
- Whether per-display fees increase at higher volumes

### Multi-Line Agencies
Agencies with multiple agents need branded display on each outbound line. Volume discounts typically kick in at 5+ lines:

| Lines | Typical Discount |
|---|---|
| 1–4 | Standard pricing |
| 5–10 | 10–15% off per-line |
| 11–25 | 15–25% off per-line |
| 25+ | Custom pricing |

### Carrier-Specific Considerations
Insurance agencies often dial both personal mobile numbers (policyholders) and business landlines (employers, carriers, agents). Ensure your provider covers both:

| Carrier Type | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Mobile (AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon) | Policyholder personal numbers |
| Business landlines | Employer groups, carrier representatives |
| VoIP/UCaaS | Internal agency lines, partner agencies |

## FAQ

**Q: What's the cheapest branded calling for a small insurance agency?**
A: CallerID Reputation at [$12–$22 per line per month](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-line) with $0 setup. The tradeoff is narrower carrier coverage than First Orion or Hiya.

**Q: Do any providers charge per-display fees?**
A: Yes. First Orion and TNS may charge $0.005–$0.02 per branded display. Hiya and CallerID Reputation typically do not. Always ask before signing.

A: Annual contracts save 10–20% but lock you in for 12 months. If you're testing branded calling for the first time, start with a month-to-month provider (CallerID Reputation or Prestyj) to validate ROI before committing.
