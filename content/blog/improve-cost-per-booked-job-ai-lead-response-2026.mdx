## The Cost Per Booked Job Formula

Most businesses stop at cost per lead. Here's the full math:

```text
Cost per booked job = Total ad spend ÷ (Total leads × Lead-to-booking conversion rate)
```

**Example:**

| Input | Before AI | After AI |
|---|---:|---:|
| Monthly ad spend | $10,000 | $10,000 |
| Leads generated | 200 | 200 |
| Lead-to-booking rate | 5% | 15% |
| Jobs booked | 10 | 30 |
| **Cost per booked job** | **$1,000** | **$333** |

Same ad spend. Same lead volume. **67% lower cost per booked job.** The only variable that changed was how fast and consistently those leads were contacted and followed up.

## ROI Calculation: Is It Worth It?

Use this framework:

```text
Monthly AI benefit = (new bookings - old bookings) × average job profit
Monthly AI cost = platform subscription + setup (amortized)
Monthly net benefit = Monthly AI benefit - Monthly AI cost
ROI = (Monthly net benefit × 12) / (Annual AI cost) × 100
```
