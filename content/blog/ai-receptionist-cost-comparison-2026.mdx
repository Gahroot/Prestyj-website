## The Hidden Costs of Human Answering Services

| Hidden cost | Annual impact (at 500 calls/month) |
|---|---:|
| After-hours premium (40% of calls) | $2,400–$6,000 |
| Holiday rates (10 days/year at 2x) | $800–$2,000 |
| Overage minutes (20% of months) | $1,200–$3,000 |
| Manual data entry (500 calls × 10 min × $25/hr) | $20,833 |
| Missed bookings (lower conversion rate) | $15,000–$40,000 in lost revenue |
| **Total hidden cost** | **$40,233–$51,833** |

The line item most businesses miss: **manual data entry.** Human answering services send SMS or email summaries that someone in your office must manually enter into your CRM, ServiceTitan, or calendar. At 8–15 minutes per call, that's a full-time job's worth of admin labor.

## FAQ

**Q: Will callers know they're talking to an AI?**
A: Modern AI receptionists handle natural conversation. Most callers can't tell the difference. For the 2–5% of high-emotion calls that need human empathy, AI platforms can transfer to a live agent.

**Q: Can an AI receptionist handle emergency calls?**
A: Yes. AI receptionists can be configured to recognize emergency language, collect critical information, and immediately dispatch to the appropriate team or emergency services. Many platforms offer this out of the box.

**Q: What about after-hours coverage?**
A: This is where AI receptionists have the biggest advantage. After-hours coverage is included in the monthly fee, while human answering services charge 1.5–2x premium rates for nights, weekends, and holidays.

**Q: How do I switch from my current answering service?**
A: Most AI receptionist platforms can be configured and live within 3–7 days. You'll need to provide your call routing rules, common call scenarios, and CRM/calendar access. The transition is typically seamless.
