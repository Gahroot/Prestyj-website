AI voice agent pricing looks simple until you add up the real costs. A platform advertising "$0.08/minute" might actually cost $0.30–$0.50/minute once you factor in phone number fees, telephony charges, concurrency limits, and the 20–40 hours of engineering time needed to configure it properly.

This comparison breaks down the true cost of the five leading AI voice agent platforms at realistic call volumes for service businesses — not just the sticker price.

---

**TL;DR:** AI voice agent platforms range from **$0.08–$0.25/minute** in raw usage to **$0.20–$0.50/minute fully loaded** including telephony, setup, and management. At 1,000 calls/month (avg 4 minutes each), total platform cost ranges from **$400–$2,000/month** — but the fully-loaded cost including setup amortization, management time, and integration maintenance pushes the real number to **$800–$3,500/month**. Done-for-you platforms like Prestyj charge **$799–$1,499/month** all-in, eliminating the hidden cost layers. The cheapest platform is the one that delivers the lowest cost per *booked appointment*, not the lowest cost per minute.

AI voice platforms land at **$0.94–$2.50 per booked call** when fully loaded, while done-for-you managed services land at **$0.94–$1.75 per booked call** because they eliminate the setup and management cost layers. ## Key Takeaways

- **Raw per-minute cost is misleading** — add telephony ($0.01–$0.03/min), phone numbers ($1–$5/month each), and management time to get the real number
- **At 1,000 calls/month (4,000 minutes), fully-loaded costs range $800–$3,500/month** depending on platform and management approach
- **Done-for-you platforms eliminate hidden costs** — one monthly fee covers everything including setup, optimization, and management
- **The right metric is cost per booked appointment** — ranges from $0.94–$2.50 across platforms
- **Platform choice depends on technical resources** — developer platforms (Bland, Retell) need engineering time; no-code platforms (Synthflow) need configuration time; done-for-you (Prestyj) needs neither

## FAQ

**Q: Which platform is cheapest per minute?**
A: Bland AI at $0.07–$0.09/minute. But per-minute cost is misleading — add telephony, phone numbers, setup, and management time to get the real cost.

**Q: Can I switch platforms later if I start with DIY?**
A: Yes, but expect 2–4 weeks of reconfiguration and $2,000–$5,000 in migration costs. Starting with done-for-you and switching to DIY later (if you want more control) is lower risk.

**Q: What's the minimum call volume to justify an AI voice platform?**
A: Most platforms break even vs a human answering service at 200+ calls/month. Below that, the math still works but the savings are smaller.

**Q: How do I compare platforms I can't test side-by-side?**
A: Request a demo call with each platform's AI agent. Test with a real inbound call scenario. Measure: response speed, conversation quality, booking accuracy, and post-call follow-up.
