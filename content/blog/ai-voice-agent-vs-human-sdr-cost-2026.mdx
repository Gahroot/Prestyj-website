## When AI Wins

- **Inbound lead response:** AI responds in seconds; humans respond in hours. [78% of customers hire the first responder](/stat/stl-78pct).
- **High-volume outbound:** AI makes [46,000+ calls/month](/blog/ai-make-46000-calls-per-month-without-hiring-sdrs-2026); humans max out at ~3,000.
- **After-hours coverage:** AI works 24/7; humans need shifts, overtime, or an answering service.
- **Database reactivation:** AI can work through 10,000+ dormant contacts in days; humans take months.
- **Consistent qualification:** AI asks the same questions every time; humans drift, skip steps, and have bad days.

---

## When Humans Still Win

- **Complex enterprise deals:** Multi-stakeholder negotiations, relationship building, strategic nuance.
- **High-touch accounts:** When the deal size justifies human attention ($50K+ ACV).
- **Brand-sensitive interactions:** When the caller experience must be unmistakably human.
- **Creative problem-solving:** When qualification requires reading between the lines.

## The Hybrid Model (Best of Both)

Most successful teams use a hybrid model:
- **AI handles:** First contact, initial qualification, appointment booking, database reactivation, after-hours coverage
- **Humans handle:** In-person meetings, demos, negotiations, relationship management, strategic accounts

This model gives you AI efficiency at the top of the funnel and human expertise at the bottom — at 30–50% of the cost of a human-only team.

## FAQ

**How much does an AI voice agent cost compared to a human SDR?**
An AI voice agent costs $540–$3,800/month fully loaded, versus $5,700–$8,500/month for a human SDR. Cost per qualified meeting is $3–$12 (AI) vs $80–$250 (human).

**Can AI voice agents replace all human SDRs?**
For inbound lead response and high-volume outbound, yes. For complex enterprise sales and relationship-driven deals, humans remain superior. Most teams use a hybrid model.

**What about quality? Do AI-book meetings convert?**
AI-book meetings convert at similar or higher rates than human-booked meetings when the qualification criteria are well-defined. The AI asks consistent questions and doesn't skip steps.

**How fast can I deploy an AI voice agent?**
A managed AI voice agent deploys in 5–14 days. A human SDR takes 60–90 days to ramp to full productivity.
