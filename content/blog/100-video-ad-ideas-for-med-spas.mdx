## FAQs

### How many ideas should I test first?

Start with 10 ideas around one offer or buyer problem. Turn those into 100 variations before expanding into a larger matrix.

### Should the owner or spokesperson be on camera?

Usually yes. A real operator builds trust faster than generic stock creative, especially in high-consideration categories.

### What batch size should I use?

Use 100 ads for one problem lane, 300 for three lanes, 500 for the recommended active-account matrix, and 1,000 for aggressive launches or broad market testing.
