## AI Voice Agent Cost Statistics

The headline per-minute rate vendors advertise is almost never what you pay. Three numbers matter more.

- **Fully-loaded cost per minute at scale is [$0.06–$0.18](/stat/voice-agent-cost-per-minute-at-scale)** once platform, LLM, speech-to-text/text-to-speech, and telephony are included at 50,000+ minutes/month. Live answering services run $0.65–$1.20/min and in-house receptionists $0.95–$2.40/min on a loaded basis.
- **Hidden costs add [18–35%](/stat/voice-agent-hidden-cost-percent)** to the advertised monthly price — per-minute overage, telephony pass-through, integration fees, after-hours premiums, voice cloning, and managed-knowledge updates.
- **A scoped pilot sets up for [$0–$1,500](/stat/voice-agent-pilot-setup-cost-range)**, including persona configuration, knowledge ingestion, CRM webhook, and call routing — versus the $5,000–$25,000 still quoted by legacy enterprise voice vendors.

For the line-by-line vendor breakdown across Vapi, Retell, Bland, and Synthflow, see [AI voice agent costs compared](/blog/ai-voice-agent-costs-compared) and the [cost-per-minute-at-scale analysis](/blog/ai-voice-agent-cost-per-minute-at-scale-2026).

## AI Voice Agent Performance Statistics

- **AI answers [99%+ of inbound calls](/stat/mcr-ai-answer-rate)** instantly, 24/7, handling unlimited simultaneous calls — cutting missed calls about 75% versus staffed-only reception.
- **AI cuts response time from [47 hours / 8 minutes to 12–45 seconds](/stat/home-services-avg-inbound-call-response).** Home service companies average 47 hours to respond to web-form leads and over 8 minutes to answer peak-hour calls.
- **Branded calling lifts outbound answer rates [27–41%](/stat/branded-calling-answer-rate-lift)** versus unbranded numbers for home and tech services firms.
- **One AI agent places [46,000+ outbound dials/month](/stat/ai-cold-call-volume-per-agent-per-month)** — roughly the output of 35–50 human SDRs on a 9-to-5 schedule.

## The Missed-Call Problem AI Voice Agents Solve

Voice AI economics only make sense against the cost of *not* answering. The missed-call data is stark:

- **[62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered](/stat/mcr-unanswered-call-rate).** Only 37.8% reach a live person.
- **[85% of callers never call back](/stat/mcr-never-call-back)** after one unanswered attempt — they call the next business on the list.
- **[62% contact a competitor](/stat/mcr-switch-to-competitor)** instead, permanently transferring that lifetime value to a rival.
- **[30–40% of calls arrive after business hours](/stat/mcr-after-hours-call-share)**, when staffed lines roll to voicemail.
- **Inbound phone leads convert at [10–15× the rate of web-form leads](/stat/mcr-phone-lead-conversion-multiple)**, so a missed call is among the most expensive leads a business can lose.
- **[65% of customers expect an instant response](/stat/mcr-instant-response-expectation)** — an expectation voicemail and next-day callbacks structurally cannot meet.
- **The average small business loses [$126,000/year](/stat/mcr-annual-revenue-lost)** to missed calls, at $100–$1,200 per unanswered call depending on industry.
- **Automated missed-call text-back recovers up to [93% of otherwise-lost leads](/stat/mcr-text-back-recovery-rate)** when it fires within one minute.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does an AI voice agent cost per minute in 2026?

At scale (50,000+ minutes/month), AI voice agents cost a [fully-loaded $0.06–$0.18 per minute](/stat/voice-agent-cost-per-minute-at-scale) once platform, LLM, speech, and telephony are included. That compares to $0.65–$1.20/min for live answering services and $0.95–$2.40/min for in-house receptionists. Advertised rates are usually 18–35% lower than what you actually pay.

### What answer rate do AI voice agents achieve?

AI phone answering systems answer [99%+ of inbound calls](/stat/mcr-ai-answer-rate) instantly, 24/7, and handle unlimited simultaneous calls. Because they are not limited to one call at a time like a human receptionist, they remove the primary mechanical cause of missed calls during peak hours — cutting missed calls roughly 75% versus staffed-only reception.

### How much revenue do missed calls cost a small business?

The average small business loses about [$126,000 per year](/stat/mcr-annual-revenue-lost) to missed calls, with each unanswered call worth $100–$1,200 depending on industry. The problem compounds because [85% of callers never call back](/stat/mcr-never-call-back) and [62% contact a competitor](/stat/mcr-switch-to-competitor) instead.

### What does it cost to set up an AI voice agent?

A properly scoped pilot costs [$0–$1,500](/stat/voice-agent-pilot-setup-cost-range) in setup — persona configuration, knowledge ingestion, CRM webhook, and call routing — versus the $5,000–$25,000 setup fees still quoted by legacy enterprise voice vendors. Some pilots launch at $0 setup under a production commitment.

### Are AI voice agents cheaper than answering services?

Yes. At volume, AI voice agents cost [$0.06–$0.18 per minute fully loaded](/stat/voice-agent-cost-per-minute-at-scale) versus $0.65–$1.20 for live answering services — roughly a 5–10× difference — while also covering after-hours, lunch, and Monday-morning peaks that answering services bill as premium overflow. See [annual savings from switching answering services to AI](/blog/annual-savings-contractors-switching-from-answering-services-to-ai-2026).
