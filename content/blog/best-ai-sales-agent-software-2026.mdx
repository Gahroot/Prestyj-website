## The Three Categories of AI Sales Agent Software

### 1. Enterprise AI Sales Platforms ($3,000–$10,000/month)

**Conversica, Drift, 11x.ai, Regie.ai**

Built for large sales organizations with dedicated sales ops, CRM admins, and complex multi-stakeholder workflows. Deep Salesforce integration, custom conversation design, and enterprise-grade compliance. Overkill for most service businesses and real estate teams.

**When to choose:** 50+ person sales org, Salesforce-centric, dedicated sales ops team, complex B2B sales cycles.

### 2. Mid-Market Managed Platforms ($500–$5,997/month)

**Prestyj, Structurely, Verse.ai**

Built for specific industries (real estate, home services) with pre-configured conversation flows, industry-specific qualification logic, and CRM integrations that work out of the box. No engineering required. Faster deployment (days, not months).

**When to choose:** Service business or real estate team, 5–50 leads/day, want results without building a tech stack.

### 3. DIY Platforms ($59–$399/month + engineering)

**Synthflow, custom builds**

Lowest platform cost but highest hidden cost in engineering time. You build conversation flows, wire up CRM integrations, and maintain the system. Best for teams with technical resources who want maximum control.

**When to choose:** You have a developer, need custom logic, or are building AI sales into a product.
