## What Is an ISA in Real Estate?

An Inside Sales Agent (ISA) is a team member whose primary job is to respond to inbound leads, conduct initial qualification calls, and book appointments for buyer agents and listing agents.

A good ISA:
- Responds to new leads quickly (ideally within 5 minutes)
- Conducts 5–15 minute qualification conversations
- Handles objections and nurtures leads who aren't ready to move forward
- Books appointments directly onto agent calendars
- Follows up with leads over days, weeks, and months

ISAs are critical for any team spending money on ads. Without someone handling lead follow-up systematically, most ad leads go cold.

The question in 2026 is: should that "someone" be a human or an AI?

### The Turnover Problem

The dirty secret of ISA roles: they have 30–50% annual turnover. The work is repetitive, the leads are often cold and unresponsive, and the pay ceiling is limited.

Every time an ISA quits:
- Your leads go uncovered for weeks during recruiting
- You spend 2–4 weeks retraining a new hire
- Your team loses institutional knowledge and script consistency
- Your team leader loses focus on high-value activities

For a team running consistent ad spend, ISA turnover is an operational crisis that happens every 1–2 years.

## Where Human ISAs Still Win

Let's be honest: AI ISAs aren't perfect for every scenario.

**Human ISAs are better at:**
- High-emotional situations (divorce leads, estate leads, distressed sellers)
- Complex negotiations that require empathy and flexibility
- Building rapport with high-net-worth clients
- Handling very specific objections that require deep local market knowledge

**The best teams use both:** AI handles the 80% of initial outreach, qualification, and appointment booking. Human ISAs (or agents) handle the 20% of complex, high-touch conversations that genuinely need a human.

## When to Replace Your ISA with AI

Consider replacing your ISA with AI if:
- Your ISA frequently misses the 5-minute response window
- Your ISA has high turnover or you're currently between ISAs
- Your team is running paid ads but the lead-to-appointment rate is below 5%
- You want to scale ad spend but not headcount
- You're doing $20M+ in production and want a more consistent, scalable system

Keep your human ISA (or add one alongside AI) if:
- Your leads are consistently high-value and emotionally complex
- Your market requires deep local knowledge for effective qualification
- You have an exceptional ISA with low turnover and consistent performance

## The Hybrid Model: AI + Human ISA

Many top teams use AI and human ISAs together:
- **AI handles:** First response (0–60 seconds), initial qualification, appointment booking for standard leads
- **Human ISA handles:** Complex leads, high-value seller appointments, leads AI can't qualify confidently

This hybrid approach gives you:
- 24/7 coverage without full human cost
- Human touch for the deals that need it
- Maximum conversion on standard lead flow
- Reduced management burden
