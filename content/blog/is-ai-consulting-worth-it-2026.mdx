## The ROI Math: How AI Consulting Pays for Itself

### The Basic Formula

```
ROI = (Revenue Gain - AI Consultant Cost) ÷ AI Consultant Cost
```

**Revenue Gain Sources:**
- Missed calls captured → Jobs booked
- Faster response → Higher conversion rate
- After-hours availability → Business you'd otherwise miss
- Appointment reminders → Reduced no-shows
- Efficiency → Staff time saved (can handle more volume)

### Example: HVAC Company

**Before AI:**
- 300 calls/week, 25% missed = 75 missed calls
- 40% of missed calls would book if answered = 30 jobs/week lost
- Average job value: $500
- **Weekly lost revenue: 30 × $500 = $15,000**
- **Monthly lost revenue: $60,000**

**AI Investment:**
- Implementation: $15,000 (one-time)
- Monthly service: $4,000

**After AI:**
- AI captures 70% of missed calls = 52.5 additional calls answered
- 40% conversion = 21 additional jobs/week
- **Weekly additional revenue: 21 × $500 = $10,500**
- **Monthly additional revenue: $42,000**

**ROI Calculation:**
- Monthly gain: $42,000
- Monthly cost: $4,000
- **Monthly ROI: ($42,000 - $4,000) ÷ $4,000 = 950%**
- **Payback period: 5 days**

**Year 1 Total ROI:**
- Revenue gain: $42,000 × 12 = $504,000
- Total cost: $15,000 + ($4,000 × 12) = $63,000
- **Net ROI: ($504,000 - $63,000) ÷ $63,000 = 700%**

## What Determines Your ROI?

### Factor 1: Average Job Value

**High job value ($400+):**
- HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical
- Higher ROI—fewer jobs needed to cover AI cost
- Typical payback: 2-5 days

**Medium job value ($150-400):**
- Cleaning, landscaping, auto services
- Moderate ROI—need more jobs to cover cost
- Typical payback: 1-2 weeks

**Low job value (under $150):**
- Very low-margin services
- AI may not be worth it unless volume is very high
- Consider cheaper alternatives or improve margins first

### Factor 2: Missed Call Volume

**High missed calls (50+/week):**
- Massive opportunity
- AI captures significant revenue
- Very fast payback

**Medium missed calls (20-50/week):**
- Solid opportunity
- AI pays for itself quickly
- Good ROI

**Low missed calls (under 20/week):**
- Limited opportunity
- Longer payback period
- May not be worth investment

### Factor 3: After-Hours Demand

**High after-hours demand (30%+ of calls):**
- Huge competitive advantage
- Only businesses answering capture these jobs
- AI transforms after-hours into profit center

**Medium after-hours demand (10-30%):**
- Meaningful advantage
- Captures business competitors miss
- Contributes significantly to ROI

**Low after-hours demand (under 10%):**
- Limited advantage
- ROI depends more on daytime missed calls

### Factor 4: Implementation Quality

**High-quality implementation:**
- Custom workflows for your business
- Proper integration with your systems
- Ongoing optimization
- **Result: 70-90% capture rates**

**Poor implementation:**
- Cookie-cutter approach
- Weak or no integration
- No optimization
- **Result: 30-50% capture rates**

**The difference:** 2-3x ROI. Choose consultants carefully.

## Red Flags: When AI Consulting Might Not Deliver

### Red Flag 1: No Clear ROI Path

**Warning sign:** Consultant can't show you the math for your specific situation.

**What to do:** Demand ROI calculation based on your actual numbers (call volume, job value, etc.). If they can't provide it, walk away.

### Red Flag 2: Generic Implementation

**Warning sign:** Consultant uses cookie-cutter approach not customized to your business.

**What to do:** Ask for industry-specific examples and custom workflow design. Generic implementations underperform.

### Red Flag 3: No Optimization Plan

**Warning sign:** Consultant launches and disappears.

**What to do:** Ensure ongoing optimization is included. AI degrades without maintenance.

### Red Flag 4: Unrealistic Promises

**Warning sign:** Consultant promises 100% capture rate or guaranteed results.

**What to do:** Realistic capture rates: 60-80%. Anyone promising 100% is lying or inexperienced.

## Calculating Your Potential ROI

### Step 1: Count Your Missed Calls

**Method:** Check phone logs for last month. Count calls that went to voicemail or weren't answered.

**Example:** 250 calls/month went to voicemail = ~62 missed calls/week

### Step 2: Determine Your Average Job Value

**Method:** Total revenue last month ÷ number of jobs = average job value

**Example:** $80,000 revenue ÷ 200 jobs = $400/job

### Step 3: Estimate Capture Rate

**Assumption:** Quality AI implementation captures 60-80% of missed calls.

**Example:** 70% capture rate (conservative)

### Step 4: Estimate Conversion Rate

**Assumption:** 40-60% of captured calls convert to jobs.

**Example:** 50% conversion rate

### Step 5: Calculate Revenue Gain

**Formula:** Missed calls/week × capture rate × conversion rate × job value

**Example:** 62 × 70% × 50% × $400 = $8,680/week = $34,720/month

### Step 6: Subtract AI Cost

**Formula:** Revenue gain - AI cost = Net gain

**Example:** $34,720 - $3,500 = $31,220/month net gain

### Step 7: Calculate ROI

**Formula:** (Net gain ÷ AI cost) × 100 = ROI%

**Example:** ($31,220 ÷ $3,500) × 100 = **892% ROI**

### Step 8: Determine Payback Period

**Formula:** AI cost ÷ (Net gain ÷ 4.3 weeks) = Payback in weeks

**Example:** $3,500 ÷ ($31,220 ÷ 4.3) = **0.48 weeks (3.4 days)**

## FAQ: AI Consulting ROI

### What's the typical payback period?

2-8 weeks for most service businesses. High-value businesses (HVAC, roofing) pay back in days. Low-value businesses may take 2-3 months.

### What if ROI takes longer than expected?

Possible causes: Poor implementation, low job value, low call volume. Reassess implementation quality and ensure optimization is happening.

### Can you guarantee ROI?

No consultant can guarantee ROI (too many variables). But quality consultants provide realistic ROI projections based on your actual numbers and industry benchmarks.

### What if my business is unique?

If you have high job value and missed calls, AI will likely deliver ROI. If low job value or low call volume, AI may not be worth it.

### How do I measure actual ROI?

Track metrics before and after: missed calls, answer rate, conversion rate, jobs booked, revenue. Compare month-over-month.
