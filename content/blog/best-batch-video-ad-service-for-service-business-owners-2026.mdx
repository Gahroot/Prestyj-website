## Why Service Businesses Are Losing on Facebook in 2026

Three real reasons leads got more expensive:

1. **Andromeda penalizes single-creative scaling.** The plumber running the same "Need a plumber?" ad for 18 months is now paying 3x the CPM he paid in 2023.
2. **iOS privacy ate audience targeting.** Creative has to do the work targeting used to do — different hooks for different homeowner intents.
3. **Every competitor in your zip code is on Facebook now.** You're competing for the same impression supply, and the algorithm picks the advertiser with the freshest, most varied creative.

The fix isn't a better video. It's more videos.

## What Andromeda Means Specifically for Service Businesses

Meta's Andromeda update (rolled out 2024–2025) changed how the algorithm scores creative. For service businesses, the practical implications:

- **Creative diversity score matters more than targeting precision.** Running the same ad to a refined audience is less effective than running 30 different ads to a broader audience.
- **First-1.5-second hook drives 70%+ of performance variance.** What grabs attention in the scroll matters more than the rest of the video.
- **Refresh velocity is rewarded.** Accounts that swap in new creative weekly see ~30% lower CPM than accounts that refresh monthly.
- **Audience-creative match happens dynamically.** Meta now decides which creative to serve which audience automatically. Your job is to supply enough variety for the algorithm to do its job.

This is why volume strategies win for service businesses in 2026. The math is structural.

## What to Avoid in Local Video Ads

Five common mistakes that burn service-business ad budgets:

1. **Stock-music intros.** Skip the 2-second logo splash. Your ad has 1.5 seconds to earn the scroll-stop.
2. **"Quality service since 1987"** — nobody cares. Lead with the problem the homeowner has _today_.
3. **No caption.** 80%+ of homeowners watch with sound off. Captions aren't optional.
4. **Generic stock footage.** Use your trucks, your team, your jobs. Authenticity is the moat against bigger competitors.
5. **One ad running for 6 months.** That's not a strategy; that's an algorithm tax.

## The Multi-Offer Pattern That Works

Most service businesses have at least 4–6 distinct offers they could lead with:

- Emergency call (24/7 urgency)
- Free quote / inspection
- Financing / 0% APR
- Seasonal tune-up / maintenance
- New install discount
- Loyalty / repeat customer offer

A batch should test all of these. Most service businesses ride one offer too long and miss the offer-mix that would improve cost per call by 30%+.
