## Timing Optimization (When to Follow Up)

### Best Days to Follow Up

**Data from 50,000+ follow-up calls (2026):**

| Day | Contact Rate | Conversion Rate |
|-----|-------------|-----------------|
| Monday | 12% | Medium |
| Tuesday | 18% | High |
| Wednesday | 22% | **Highest** |
| Thursday | 19% | High |
| Friday | 14% | Medium |
| Saturday | 8% | Low (but less competition) |
| Sunday | 6% | Lowest |

**Insight:** Wednesday 10am-4pm is the sweet spot for B2C. Tuesday-Thursday for B2B.

### Best Times to Follow Up

**Phone:**
- 10am-12pm: High contact rate, fresh attention
- 1-3pm: Lower contact (lunch, meetings)
- 4-6pm: High contact, end-of-day availability
- After 6pm: Only for consumer (B2C) leads

**Text:**
- 9am: Morning check-in (good for appointment reminders)
- 12-1pm: Lunch break scrolling (high engagement)
- 6-8pm: Evening wind-down (highest response rate)
- Avoid: Before 8am, after 9pm (TCPA violation risk)

**Email:**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-12pm: Highest open rates
- Avoid: Friday after 3pm (weekend mode)
- Avoid: Monday before 10am (inbox overload)

**AI Advantage:** Automatically schedules follow-ups at optimal times based on lead behavior and industry benchmarks.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Too Many Touches, Too Fast

**The error:** 5 touches in 2 days

**The consequence:** Feels like harassment, high opt-out rate

**The fix:** Space touches across 14-21 days. Give time to respond between attempts.

### Mistake 2: Generic, Copy-Paste Messages

**The error:** "Hi, just following up on your inquiry"

**The consequence:** Ignored, looks like spam

**The fix:** Reference specific properties, neighborhoods, prior conversation points.

### Mistake 3: All One Channel

**The error:** 7 emails in a row, no calls or texts

**The consequence:** If they don't check email, you never connect

**The fix:** Mix channels. Different people prefer different mediums.

### Mistake 4: No Human Escalation

**The error:** AI follows up forever, never flags for human review

**The consequence:** High-value leads frustrated by robotic persistence

**The fix:** After 3-4 attempts with engagement but no booking, alert human: "Lead is engaging but not booking‚Äîreview?"

### Mistake 5: No Opt-Out Mechanism

**The error:** "Reply STOP" doesn't actually stop messages

**The consequence:** TCPA violations, spam reports, brand damage

**The fix:** Honor opt-outs instantly, every channel, every time.

## FAQ

### How many follow-up attempts should I make before giving up?

Data shows 35-50% of conversions happen between attempts 5-12. Recommended: 7-10 attempts over 21 days for high-intent leads, 4-6 attempts over 14 days for low-intent. If no engagement after that, move to long-term nurture (monthly check-ins).

### What's the best channel for follow-up: phone, text, or email?

Multi-channel works best. Phone has highest intent when they answer, text has highest response rate, email works for long-form content. Use all three in sequence: call ‚Üí text ‚Üí email ‚Üí call ‚Üí text. This approach converts 3.2x better than single-channel.

### How long should I wait between follow-up attempts?

Depends on lead intent. High-intent: attempt 1 at minute 0, attempt 2 at hour 2, attempt 3 at day 1. Medium-intent: attempt 1 at hour 1, attempt 2 at day 2, attempt 3 at day 5. Space later attempts 3-5 days apart. Never make more than 2 attempts in 24 hours.

### Can AI really personalize follow-up at scale?

Yes. Modern AI uses dynamic variables (name, property, neighborhood, budget), references prior conversation history, and adjusts messaging based on engagement patterns (opened email, clicked link, etc.). It's not perfect human-level personalization, but it's 90% as good at 5% of the cost.

### When should I stop following up with a lead?

Stop when: (1) They explicitly opt out ("not interested, please stop"), (2) After 10+ attempts with zero engagement over 30 days, or (3) They book an appointment or convert. Move cold leads to quarterly reactivation campaigns rather than deleting‚Äî15-25% can be reactivated 3-12 months later.

### How much does AI follow-up automation cost?

Typical costs: $2-8 per lead for full multi-touch sequences (call + text + email over 21 days). Compare to $27-42 per lead for human manual follow-up. For 100 leads/month, AI costs $200-800 vs $2,700-4,200 for human. Cost savings: 85-95%.
