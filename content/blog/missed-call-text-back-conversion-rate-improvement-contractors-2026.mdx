## Key Takeaways

- **Fast SMS matters:** Send the first missed-call text within **10–30 seconds**, not minutes later.
- **Reply/callback recovery:** Contractors commonly recover **18–35%** of missed calls into replies, callbacks, or booked conversations.
- **Booked appointment recovery:** A realistic benchmark is **6–14% of missed calls** becoming booked jobs or estimates.
- **AI voice follow-up:** Adding AI voice callback after SMS lifts recovery when the caller does not reply.
- **Highest ROI trades:** HVAC, plumbing, garage door, roofing, restoration, pest control, and electrical.
- **Cost per recovered lead:** Often **$2–$12**, far cheaper than buying a fresh paid lead.
- **Main failure mode:** A text-back that says “How can we help?” instead of routing to booking, urgency, and service-area qualification.

## Missed Call Text-Back Benchmarks

| Workflow | First response speed | Reply / callback recovery | Booked appointment recovery | Best fit |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| No automation | 30 min–never | 0–8% | 0–3% | Very low volume only |
| Basic auto-text | 10–60 sec | 12–25% | 4–9% | Solo contractors, small teams |
| SMS + human callback | 10–60 sec + human follow-up | 15–30% | 5–12% | Teams with dispatch capacity |
| SMS + AI voice callback | 10–30 sec + 1–3 min voice | 18–35% | 6–14% | Busy contractors and after-hours demand |
| SMS + AI + human escalation | 10–30 sec + instant routing | 22–40% | 8–18% | High-ticket and emergency trades |

The conversion lift comes from capturing the caller while intent is hot. A missed call that gets a reply in 15 seconds is not “lost” yet. A missed call returned the next day usually is.

## Why Missed Calls Are So Expensive

A missed call is not a low-intent form fill. It is a person who decided the problem was urgent enough to call.

| Trade | Typical job value | Why missed calls hurt |
|---|---:|---|
| HVAC | $250–$12,000 | Emergency no-cool/no-heat callers contact the next available contractor |
| Plumbing | $200–$5,000 | Leak, clog, and water-heater calls are time-sensitive |
| Roofing | $500–$20,000+ | Storm calls come in waves and callers need fast inspection slots |
| Garage door | $180–$1,500 | Broken-door callers usually book whoever answers first |
| Restoration | $2,500–$25,000+ | Water/fire damage calls are urgent and high-value |
| Pest control | $150–$900 | Seasonal spikes and infestation urgency drive fast decisions |
| Electrical | $250–$8,000 | Panel, outage, and generator calls require fast trust-building |

If a $60 Google Ads click produces a phone call and the call is missed, the ad spend is not the only loss. The lost opportunity may be 10–100x the click cost.

## The Best Missed-Call Workflow

| Step | Timing | Message / action | Goal |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0–10 sec | Detect missed call and match number to CRM | Avoid duplicate / wrong context |
| 2 | 10–30 sec | Send short text from business number | Recover attention immediately |
| 3 | 1–3 min | AI voice callback if no reply | Offer booking while intent is fresh |
| 4 | 3–5 min | Qualify service area, urgency, job type | Separate emergencies from routine work |
| 5 | Instant | Book appointment or dispatch slot | Convert into calendar event |
| 6 | Instant | Human escalation for emergency/angry/VIP | Preserve trust where judgment matters |
| 7 | Same day | Follow-up if no response | Capture delayed replies |

The first message should be specific and useful, not robotic.

**Weak text:** “Sorry we missed your call. How can we help?”

**Better text:** “This is Prestyj Plumbing — sorry we missed you. Are you calling about an emergency or a regular appointment? Reply 1 for emergency, 2 for booking, or call back here.”

The missed-call game is won in the first minute. After that, the workflow becomes follow-up rather than conversion rescue.

## What Vendors Don’t Tell You

1. **A text-back is not a booking system.** It needs routing, qualification, and calendar/dispatch connection.
2. **The first 30 seconds matter more than the copy.** A perfect text sent 45 minutes late loses.
3. **Generic auto-replies underperform.** Use trade, urgency, and next-step language.
4. **After-hours missed calls need different logic.** The customer may need emergency dispatch, not tomorrow’s office callback.
5. **Human escalation must be real.** If the AI detects anger, emergency, or a VIP customer, someone needs to get the alert.
6. **Measure booked recovery, not just replies.** Replies that do not become appointments are not the goal.

## FAQ

### What is missed-call text-back automation?

Missed-call text-back automation sends an immediate SMS when a business misses an inbound phone call. A stronger version can also trigger AI voice callback, qualify the caller, book an appointment, and notify a human for urgent calls.

### How much does missed-call text-back improve conversion?

Contractors commonly recover **18–35%** of missed calls into replies or callbacks and **6–14%** into booked appointments when SMS is paired with fast follow-up and booking logic.

### How fast should the text-back be sent?

The first text should be sent within **10–30 seconds**. Waiting five minutes or more sharply reduces recovery because the caller may already be talking to a competitor.

### Is SMS enough, or should contractors use AI voice too?

SMS is enough for low-volume routine missed calls. SMS plus AI voice is better for after-hours, emergency trades, high-ticket calls, and businesses that miss enough calls to justify automated callback.

### What should the missed-call text say?

It should identify the business, apologize briefly, and offer a clear next step. For example: “Sorry we missed you — is this an emergency or a regular appointment? Reply 1 for emergency, 2 for booking.”

### Does missed-call text-back work after hours?

Yes, after-hours is one of the strongest use cases. The system can identify emergencies, book non-urgent appointments, and escalate urgent calls to an on-call human.

### What trades benefit most from missed-call automation?

HVAC, plumbing, garage door, roofing, restoration, pest control, electrical, and other urgent home services benefit most because callers often hire whoever responds first.

### How much does missed-call text-back cost?

Basic SMS tools can cost under a few hundred dollars per month. AI voice and managed workflows can cost more, but cost per recovered booked call often remains far below buying a new paid lead.

### Is missed-call text-back compliant?

It can be, but businesses need opt-out language, consent review, proper identification, quiet-hour rules, and TCPA-aware workflows. Compliance should be reviewed before automating SMS or callbacks.

### What metric matters most?

Booked recovered appointments matter more than reply rate. Track missed calls, text replies, AI callback connects, booked appointments, revenue, and cost per recovered job.
