Follow Up Boss is a great real estate CRM for team management and pipeline tracking, but its built-in AI capabilities are limited. ## What Follow Up Boss Does Well

Follow Up Boss is one of the most widely used real estate CRMs for teams and brokerages — and for good reason:

- **Pipeline visibility**: See every lead, every stage, every agent, in one dashboard
- **Team management**: Route leads, assign agents, track follow-up activity
- **Third-party integrations**: 200+ integrations with real estate platforms
- **Smart lists**: Segment your database by lead source, stage, agent, and more
- **Calling and texting**: Built-in dialer and text messaging
- **Reporting**: Track agent activity, conversion rates, response times

If you're a team doing $10M+ in production, Follow Up Boss is an excellent foundation for your lead management system.

## What Follow Up Boss Doesn't Do (Well)

Here's what FUB can't do on its own — and why teams need AI:

### Instant AI Response

Follow Up Boss can send automated emails when a new lead comes in. But it can't:
- Respond to that lead in under 60 seconds, automatically
- Have a real two-way conversation via SMS
- Qualify the lead through a natural dialogue
- Book an appointment without human involvement

FUB's action plans (automated sequences) are one-directional drip campaigns. They send emails and texts on a schedule — but they can't respond intelligently to what the lead says back.

### 24/7 Conversational Follow-Up

FUB will alert your agents when a new lead comes in, but it can't engage that lead at 2am on a Saturday. If your team isn't available, the lead waits — and your conversion rate drops.

### AI Lead Qualification

FUB can score leads based on behavior (website activity, email opens), but it can't conduct a qualification conversation. That still requires a human — or an AI that can talk to the lead directly.

## Setting Up the Prestyj + Follow Up Boss Integration

Here's how the integration works step by step:

### Step 1: Connect via Webhook or API

Follow Up Boss supports webhooks that fire when new leads enter the system. Prestyj subscribes to these webhooks to receive lead data in real time.

Alternatively, Prestyj integrates via the Follow Up Boss API — syncing data bidirectionally.

### Step 2: Configure Routing Rules

In Prestyj, set up routing logic that mirrors your FUB team structure:
- Route buyer leads to buyer agents
- Route seller leads to listing agents
- Set up geographic territory routing
- Configure round-robin or availability-based distribution

### Step 3: Train the AI

Prestyj's setup team trains the AI on your brokerage's specifics:
- Your brand voice and team name
- Your qualification criteria (what makes a "hot" lead)
- Your market-specific talking points
- Objection handling scripts

### Step 4: Sync Conversations Back to FUB

Every AI conversation syncs back to the FUB lead record as a note. Agents see the full conversation history when they're notified of a hot lead.

### Step 5: Calendar Integration

Prestyj connects to each agent's calendar (Google Calendar or Outlook). When AI books an appointment, it blocks on both the agent's calendar and appears in their FUB pipeline.

## Best Practices for Follow Up Boss + AI

### Let AI Be First Responder on All New Leads

Don't use AI only for overflow or after-hours coverage. Let AI respond to every new lead immediately — that's where the maximum conversion lift comes from.

### Set Clear Hot Lead Criteria

Define exactly what makes a lead worth an immediate agent notification:
- Pre-approved buyer wanting to move within 90 days
- Seller ready to list within 60 days
- Specific price range or property type

Configure Prestyj to flag these criteria and notify the assigned agent in FUB immediately.

### Don't Let FUB Action Plans Conflict with AI

Coordinate your FUB action plans with your Prestyj AI sequences to avoid sending conflicting messages. Typically: let AI handle the first 14 days of SMS follow-up, then let FUB email sequences take over for longer-term nurture.

### Review AI Conversations in FUB Weekly

Every week, have your team leader review a sample of AI conversations that synced to FUB. This helps identify:
- Qualification questions to add or adjust
- Objection handling gaps
- Leads that were misrouted

## Common Questions About Follow Up Boss + AI

**Does Prestyj replace Follow Up Boss?**

No. Prestyj enhances FUB. FUB remains your CRM for pipeline management, agent tracking, and team visibility. Prestyj adds the AI response and qualification layer on top.

**Will agents still use Follow Up Boss normally?**

Yes. Agents work in FUB as always. Prestyj adds AI notes and hot-lead notifications to their existing FUB workflow.

The AI conversation history is in FUB, so whoever answers the call can immediately see the full context. Warm handoffs are seamless.

**Can AI handle Follow Up Boss smart list actions?**

Yes. Prestyj can be triggered by specific FUB smart list criteria — for example, starting an AI outreach campaign when a lead hasn't been contacted in 30 days.
