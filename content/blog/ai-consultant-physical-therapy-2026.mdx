## What AI Does for PT Clinics

### 1. Automated Appointment Scheduling

**New patient scheduling:**
- AI collects key information: chief complaint, insurance, availability
- Matches patient to appropriate therapist (specialties, availability)
- Schedules 60-90 minute initial evaluation slots
- Sends confirmation with prep instructions

**Follow-up scheduling:**
- AI schedules at end of treatment (therapist recommends frequency)
- Finds slots matching patient preferences and therapist availability
- Handles recurring appointment scheduling
- Confirms insurance coverage before booking

**Cancellation and rescheduling:**
- 24/7 cancellation line (no waiting for office to open)
- Immediate rescheduling when cancellations open slots
- Waitlist management for high-demand times
- Minimizes gaps in therapist schedules

### 2. Patient Reminders and Confirmation

**Appointment reminders:**
- 48-hour advance reminder via SMS and email
- 24-hour reminder via preferred channel
- Response required to confirm ("reply Y to confirm")
- Automatic rescheduling if patient can't make it

**No-show reduction tactics:**
- "Your appointment is tomorrow at [time]. Reply CONFIRM or RESCHEDULE."
- "We have a waitlist for this time. Please confirm or we'll offer your slot."
- Multiple reminder channels (SMS + email + voice if preferred)

**Prep instructions:**
- "Wear loose clothing for knee evaluation"
- "Bring MRI reports if available"
- "Arrive 15 minutes early for paperwork"
- "Complete intake forms online before arrival"

### 3. Insurance Verification Automation

**Eligibility checks:**
- Automated verification before each appointment
- Checks coverage status, copay, deductible
- Flags authorization requirements
- Identifies visit limits approaching

**Pre-authorization:**
- Identifies treatments requiring pre-authorization
- Initiates authorization requests automatically
- Tracks approval status
- Alerts staff if authorization not received before appointment

**Patient communication:**
- "Your insurance covers PT with $25 copay"
- "You've had 8 visits this year (20 allowed)"
- "Pre-authorization approved for 12 visits"

### 4. Patient Intake Automation

**New patient forms:**
- AI collects basic information via phone or web form
- Medical history, current medications, prior treatments
- Current complaints, pain levels, functional limitations
- Insurance card photo collection via SMS link

**Day-of-appointment prep:**
- "Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete remaining paperwork"
- "Wear comfortable clothing for movement assessment"
- "Bring list of current medications"
- "Complete these 3 forms before arrival: [link]"

### 5. After-Hours Call Handling

**Patient questions:**
- "What time is my appointment tomorrow?" → AI answers instantly
- "What's your address?" → AI provides directions
- "Do you take [insurance]?" → AI verifies and answers
- "Can I reschedule my 3 PM appointment?" → AI handles it

**New patient calls:**
- Collects basic information
- Schedules initial evaluation
- Sends confirmation and prep instructions
- Adds to practice management system

**Emergency triage:**
- "I fell and think I broke my hip" → "Please go to urgent care or ER"
- Post-surgical questions → Flags for therapist follow-up
- Pain concerns → Assesses urgency and schedules appropriately

## PT-Specific AI Features

### Evaluation Scheduling

**Initial evaluation considerations:**
- 60-90 minute time blocks
- Therapist specialties (orthopedic, neurological, pediatric, etc.)
- Equipment requirements (treatment tables, parallel bars, etc.)
- New patient vs. existing patient scheduling

**Treatment scheduling:**
- 30-60 minute follow-up slots
- Frequency matching (3x/week, 2x/week, etc.)
- Recurring appointment rules
- Therapist consistency (same therapist when possible)

### Insurance Workflow

**Commercial insurance:**
- Verify eligibility and coverage
- Check copay/coinsurance
- Identify deductible status
- Flag pre-authorization requirements

**Medicare:**
- Verify Medicare eligibility
- Check therapy cap status
- Document medical necessity requirements
- Track visit counts toward caps

**Medicaid:**
- Verify state Medicaid eligibility
- Check authorization requirements
- Document referral requirements
- Track visit limits

### HIPAA Compliance

- Secure data transmission
- Encrypted storage
- Business associate agreements in place

**Privacy protection:**
- Limited information collection
- Secure authentication for accessing records
