## How the 46,000 Number Is Built

**24 hours × ~32 days × parallel sessions × ~1 dial / 90 seconds** under compliant local-time windows = ~46,000 dial attempts per month per AI agent instance. The math conservatively assumes:

- Local-time dialing windows (typically 8 a.m. – 9 p.m. recipient local time)
- DNC scrubbing and consent gates active
- Cool-down between same-number attempts (typically 4–7 days between contact attempts on no-answer)
- One concurrent call session per agent instance

Run 2 instances and you're at 90k+ dials/month. Run 10 instances and you're past 450k — at a fraction of the loaded cost of the equivalent SDR team.

## Where AI Cold Calling Beats Humans

- **First-touch qualification at volume.** AI is faster, more consistent, and never misses a follow-up cadence.
- **Reactivating dormant lead lists.** A 50,000-contact dormant list takes a 5-rep SDR team 60–90 days to work end-to-end. An AI agent completes it in 5–10 days.
- **Time-of-day coverage.** AI dials in evening and weekend windows where most SDR teams aren't staffed.
- **Compliance auditability.** Every call recorded, disposition-coded, and timestamped — far cleaner than typical SDR notes.

## Where Humans Still Win

- **Live demo and discovery on enterprise opportunities.**
- **Complex objection handling on six-figure deals.**
- **Relationship continuity with named accounts over months.**

The right architecture is AI for first touch + qualification + meeting set, humans for live qualification call and close.

## FAQ

**Q: Is 46,000 dials/month TCPA-compliant?**
A: Yes — provided the AI is operating within local-time windows, DNC-scrubbed, and properly consent-gated. Volume itself isn't the compliance trigger; manner and consent are.

**Q: What's the connect rate?**
A: With branded calling, expect [27–41% higher answer rates](/stat/branded-calling-answer-rate-lift) than unbranded. Typical connect rates land 8–18% across cold lists, much higher on warmer reactivation lists.

**Q: Does the AI handle objections?**
A: Modern AI agents handle the common 20–30 objections by name with scripted-but-natural responses. Anything off-script gets warm-transferred to a human SDR.
