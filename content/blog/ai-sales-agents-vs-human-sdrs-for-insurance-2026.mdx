## Where AI Converts Better for Insurance

### 1. Quote Follow-Up
AI follows up on new quotes within minutes — calling, texting, and emailing until the prospect responds. This recovers 15–25% of quotes that would otherwise go cold.

### 2. Cross-Sell Campaigns
AI can identify clients with only auto who could add home, or clients with one property who could add umbrella — and reach out automatically.

### 3. Policy Renewal Outreach
AI contacts policyholders 30–60 days before renewal, confirms coverage needs, and schedules reviews — reducing churn.

### 4. Referral Program Activation
AI can contact past clients and ask for referrals — generating warm leads without manual effort.

## Where Human SDRs Still Win

### 1. Complex Coverage Consultations
Commercial insurance, high-net-worth personal lines, and specialty coverage require human expertise.

### 2. Claims Situations
Claims follow-up and dispute resolution need human judgment and empathy.

### 3. Relationship Management
Large accounts, agency-of-record relationships, and referral partnerships benefit from personal touch.
