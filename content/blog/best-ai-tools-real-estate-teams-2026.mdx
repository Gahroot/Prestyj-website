## Category 2: AI Appointment Setting

**What it does:** Automatically books qualified leads into appointments on agent calendars — without any human coordination.

**Why it matters:** The bottleneck between "qualified lead" and "booked appointment" is massive for most teams. AI appointment setting eliminates the back-and-forth, the missed calls, and the no-shows from unconfirmed appointments.

### How It Works

Good AI appointment setting:
1. Qualifies the lead conversationally
2. Offers available calendar slots from the assigned agent
3. Confirms the appointment via SMS + email
4. Sends reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before
5. Handles reschedule requests automatically

### Top Options

Most AI lead response platforms (including Prestyj) include AI appointment setting. If you're evaluating AI lead response, look for one that handles the full funnel from response → qualification → booking.

**Who needs it:** Teams booking 10+ appointments per month where scheduling coordination is a bottleneck.

## Category 4: AI CRM Automation

**What it does:** Keeps your CRM database active by automatically triggering actions based on lead behavior, time since last contact, and stage in your pipeline.

**Why it matters:** The average real estate team has thousands of leads sitting in their CRM with no follow-up plan. AI CRM automation turns this dormant database into a revenue-generating machine.

### Key Features

- **Behavioral triggers:** Automatically follow up when a lead views a listing, opens an email, or visits your website
- **Time-based sequences:** Automatically reach out to leads who haven't been contacted in 30/60/90 days
- **Lead scoring:** Prioritize your most engaged leads automatically
- **Dead lead reactivation:** Run periodic campaigns to re-engage leads that have gone cold

### Top Options for Real Estate

**Follow Up Boss** — Best CRM for real estate teams with strong automation features. Not AI-native, but supports integrations with AI tools like Prestyj.

**kvCORE** — All-in-one platform with built-in lead generation, IDX, and CRM automation. More comprehensive than FUB but also more complex.

**Sierra Interactive** — Strong IDX platform with behavioral tracking and CRM features. Good for teams with significant website traffic.

**Prestyj + Any CRM** — Adding Prestyj's AI on top of your existing CRM adds the conversational AI layer that most CRMs lack.

**Who needs it:** Any team with more than 500 leads in their database who isn't working them systematically.

## What Real Estate AI Tools Are NOT Worth It

**AI "predictive" tools that promise to predict who will sell** — These are generally not worth the cost for most teams. The data quality is inconsistent and the conversion rate on "predicted sellers" is typically very low.

**AI valuation tools marketed to consumers** — Products like Opendoor's instant offer or Offerpad have commoditized the "instant valuation" hook. Building your own AI valuation tool is rarely worth the investment.

**Generic chatbots on your website** — If the chatbot isn't specifically trained on real estate conversations and integrated with your CRM, it'll frustrate leads more than convert them.

## How to Prioritize AI for Your Team

If you're just starting to add AI to your real estate team:

1. **Start with AI lead response.** This is the highest ROI AI investment — period. Fix your speed to lead before anything else.

2. **Add AI appointment setting.** Once leads are being engaged, make sure appointments are booking automatically.

3. **Systematize your CRM.** Make sure your entire database is being worked, not just fresh leads.

4. **Add AI content tools.** Once operations are optimized, use AI to scale your marketing output.

5. **Evaluate advanced tools.** Market analysis, predictive tools, and specialized platforms come after the foundation is solid.
