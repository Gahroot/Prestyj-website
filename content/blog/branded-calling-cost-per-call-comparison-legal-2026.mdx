Law firms comparing branded calling providers often focus on the per-line monthly fee — but the number that actually matters is **cost per connected conversation**. A $12/line provider with narrow carrier coverage may cost more per answer than a $35/line provider with deeper reach. This post breaks down the real cost per call across every major branded calling provider for legal outbound.

**TL;DR:** Branded calling cost per connected call for legal firms ranges from [$0.02–$0.11](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-connected-call) depending on provider and dial volume. At 2,000+ dials/month per line, CallerID Reputation is cheapest per line ($12–$22) but may cost more per answer due to narrower carrier coverage. First Orion ($25–$45/line) delivers the lowest cost per answer at scale because deeper carrier coverage means more displays render. Bundled voice-agent providers eliminate the separate line fee entirely.

**Direct answer:** The cheapest branded calling provider per connected call for legal firms depends on your carrier mix and dial volume. For solo practitioners under 1,500 dials/month, CallerID Reputation has the lowest total cost. For mid-size firms at 1,500–10,000 dials/month, First Orion or Hiya delivers lower cost per answer. For high-volume operations, bundled providers win on both cost and simplicity. See the [Prestyj platform](/platform) or [book a pricing review](/book-demo) for a custom comparison.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cost per connected call ranges from [$0.02–$0.11](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-connected-call)** across providers — the per-line fee alone doesn't tell you which is cheapest.
- **Carrier coverage directly impacts cost per answer** — a provider that displays on 90% of carriers connects more conversations per dollar than one that displays on 60%.
- **At 2,000+ dials/month per line, the math favors deeper coverage** — the incremental cost of a higher per-line fee is offset by more connected conversations.
- **Bundled providers eliminate the separate line fee** — cost per minute includes branded display, simplifying the comparison.
- **Per-display micro-fees** ($0.005–$0.02 each) can add $80–$300/month at scale — ask every provider about display fees.
- **The right comparison is total campaign cost per landed client**, not per-line cost.

## How to Calculate Your Actual Cost Per Answer

```text
Cost per connected call = (monthly line fee + monthly display fees) / (total dials × answer rate × display rate)
```

Example for a mid-size law firm:

| Input | CallerID Reputation | First Orion | Bundled (Prestyj) |

## Hidden Costs in Cost-Per-Call Calculations

- **Per-display micro-fees** ($0.005–$0.02 each) — some providers charge these on top of the per-line fee.
- **Attestation setup fees** ($0–$500 one-time) — amortize over 12 months for true monthly cost.
- **Carrier coverage changes** — coverage can change as carriers update their databases.
- **Logo approval delays** — 5–15 business days for branded name/logo approval.

## FAQ

**Q: What's the cheapest branded calling per call for law firms?**
A: At low volume, CallerID Reputation at [$12–$22/line](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-line). At mid-volume, First Orion or Hiya delivers lower cost per answer.

**Q: How do I calculate my actual cost per connected call?**
A: Divide total monthly branded calling cost by calls that connect with your brand displayed. Formula: (monthly cost) / (total dials × answer rate × carrier display rate).

**Q: Does carrier coverage matter that much for law firms?**
A: Yes. A provider that displays on 70% of carriers connects 30% fewer conversations than one at 95%. At 2,000 dials/month, that's 150 fewer conversations — potentially 7+ fewer landed clients.
