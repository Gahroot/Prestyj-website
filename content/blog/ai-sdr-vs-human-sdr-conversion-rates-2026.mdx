**AI responds in 12–45 seconds.** Human SDRs, even well-managed ones, average 4–8 hours on inbound leads and 24–72 hours on outbound follow-up. The math is simple: the first business to respond frames the entire buying process. 78% of buyers go with whoever responds first.

AI doesn't choose between email, voice, SMS, and LinkedIn. It runs all four simultaneously, optimizing for the channel each prospect responds to fastest.

A human SDR choosing between channels is making a tradeoff. AI running all channels simultaneously is creating optionality. The prospect who ignores email might respond to a text in 90 seconds.
