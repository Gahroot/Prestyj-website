## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does an AI sales agent cost compared to a human?

Human Inside Sales Associates cost $67,500/year total compensation (including salary, benefits, taxes). AI sales agent systems cost $28,000/year (platform + per-lead fees). The hybrid model (AI + reduced human role) costs $42,000/year and delivers the best performance: 4.8% conversion vs. 1.2% for human-only and 4.1% for AI-only.

### Do AI sales agents replace human sales teams?

No — and they shouldn't. The data shows hybrid approaches (AI + human) convert 4.8% of leads compared to 1.2% for human-only and 4.1% for AI-only. AI handles repetitive, time-sensitive tasks (instant response, qualification) while humans handle relationship building, judgment, and closing. Companies replacing humans entirely with AI see 31% success rates vs. 79% for hybrid models.

### What is the ROI of AI vs. human sales agents?

AI delivers 312% ROI; human ISAs deliver 87% ROI; hybrid delivers 687% ROI — hybrid wins by 3.4x. For a business handling 200 leads/month with $12,000 average sale: AI generates $1.148M net revenue, humans generate $280.5K, hybrid generates $1.338M. The hybrid model costs $42,000/year and generates 115 closings/year vs. 29 for humans.

### How fast do AI sales agents respond to leads?

AI systems respond in 47 seconds on average; human ISAs respond in 47 hours on average. This 5,000x speed difference matters: responding within 1 minute converts 4.5% of leads, while responding after 24 hours converts 0.8%. Additionally, AI provides 24/7 coverage while humans cover only 23% of weekly hours — meaning humans miss 77% of leads that arrive after hours.

### What's the cost per lead for AI vs. human sales agents?

Human ISAs cost $104 per lead engaged; AI systems cost $13 per lead engaged. The hybrid model costs $19 per lead engaged. Per closed deal: humans cost $2,328, AI costs $286, hybrid costs $365. AI is 6-8x more cost-effective per lead and per closed deal.

### When should I choose human ISAs over AI?

Choose human ISAs only if: lead volume is under 50/month, sales are highly complex requiring deep expertise, all leads arrive during business hours, you have unlimited budget and hiring timeline, and missing 77% of after-hours leads is acceptable. Fewer than 5% of businesses fit this profile. Most businesses benefit from AI or hybrid approaches.

### What's the break-even point for AI vs. human sales agents?

AI becomes cheaper than human ISAs at 75+ leads per month. At 200 leads/month (common for growing businesses), AI costs $28,000/year vs. $67,500 for one human ISA. The break-even occurs in month 4: by month 12, AI has saved $69,586 compared to human ISAs. For scaling beyond 400 leads/month, AI costs 7-10x less than hiring multiple ISAs.

### How do I calculate ROI for my specific business?

Calculate your ROI: (Annual revenue - Annual cost) ÷ Annual cost × 100. For AI with 200 leads/month, $12,000 average sale, 4.1% conversion: Revenue = ($12,000 × 200 × 12 × 0.041) = $1.176M. Cost = $28,000. ROI = (($1.176M - $28,000) ÷ $28,000) × 100 = 3,100% (31x return). Plug in your actual lead volume, average sale amount, and conversion rates to see your specific ROI.
