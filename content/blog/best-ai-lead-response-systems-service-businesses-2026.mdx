## The Three Categories of AI Lead Response Systems

### 1. Voice-First (Best for Home Services)

Optimized for inbound phone calls and outbound AI callbacks within 60 seconds of form submission. Tight integration with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, FieldEdge. Best fit for HVAC, plumbing, roofing, garage door, electrical, pest control, pool service.

### 2. Form-First (Best for B2B Services)

Optimized for web form, chat widget, and email triggers. Heavier weight on qualification logic, calendar booking, and CRM enrichment (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive). Best fit for legal, financial advisory, IT services, marketing agencies.

### 3. Hybrid (Best for High-Volume Mixed Lead Sources)

Combines voice + SMS + email + form triggers under a single workflow. Best for multi-location service operators with paid lead spend across Google Search, Meta, lead aggregators, and organic.

## Questions to Ask Before Signing

1. **What's your fully-loaded cost per engaged lead at our expected volume?** (Not subscription — fully loaded.)
2. **Does your CRM write-back land in the same record the lead lives in, or create a duplicate?**
3. **What's your after-hours coverage and how is it priced?**
4. **Do you include branded calling on outbound callbacks?**
5. **Can you show me three customer references in our exact vertical?**
6. **What happens to the contract if our inbound volume doubles? Halves?**

## FAQ

**Q: Is AI lead response a replacement for an ISA team?**
A: For most home services SMBs (under 50 leads/day) it's a replacement. For high-volume operators (over 200 leads/day), it's the first layer with humans handling escalations.

**Q: Do AI lead response systems work with paid lead sources like Angi / Thumbtack?**
A: Yes — most integrate via webhook or email-parser. Speed-to-lead on paid lead-aggregator inbound is where the ROI is most dramatic.

**Q: What's the ramp time?**
