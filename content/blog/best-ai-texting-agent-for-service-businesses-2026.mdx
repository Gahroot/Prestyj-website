## The Three Categories of AI Texting Platforms

### 1. AI-First Conversational Platforms (Best for Lead Qualification)

**Prestyj, Verse.ai**

These platforms use true conversational AI to conduct two-way SMS conversations with leads. The AI reads responses, adapts its questions, qualifies the lead, and books appointments or routes to humans. This is fundamentally different from scheduled drip campaigns.

**When to choose:** You have inbound leads (from ads, website, lead aggregators) that need immediate, intelligent follow-up. Speed-to-lead and qualification depth are your priorities.

### 2. SMS Automation Tools (Best for Broadcasts and Reminders)

**Aloware, MessageDesk, Textel, Skipio**

These platforms automate SMS sequences — appointment reminders, review requests, seasonal promotions, drip campaigns. Some have basic AI for auto-responses, but they're not designed for real-time conversational qualification.

**When to choose:** You need appointment reminders, review follow-up, and broadcast messaging. You don't need real-time lead qualification via SMS.

### 3. SMS Broadcast Tools (Best for One-Way Mass Messaging)

**EZ Texting**

One-way SMS blast tools for mass promotions and alerts. No two-way conversation, no AI, no lead qualification.

**When to choose:** You need to send mass SMS promotions or alerts to your existing database.

## FAQ

**What's the difference between AI texting and SMS automation?**
AI texting uses conversational AI to conduct two-way conversations — it reads responses, adapts questions, and qualifies leads. SMS automation sends pre-written drip sequences on a schedule. AI texting qualifies; SMS automation broadcasts.

**How fast should an AI texting agent respond?**
Under 60 seconds for inbound leads. Every minute of delay reduces qualification odds by [8x](/stat/stl-8x-drop). The best platforms respond in 12–45 seconds.

**Is AI texting compliant with TCPA?**
Yes, if the platform handles 10DLC registration, opt-out management, and consent tracking. All platforms on this list offer basic compliance, but verify state-level DNC handling for your market.

**Can AI texting replace my ISA team?**
For most service businesses (under 50 leads/day), yes. For high-volume operators (200+ leads/day), AI texting is the first layer with humans handling escalations.
