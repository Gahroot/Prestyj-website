**Inventory management:**
- Low stock alerts
- Automated reorder triggers

### 4. Shift Handoff Automation

**Automated documentation:**
- Production totals and targets
- Issues encountered and resolutions
- Equipment status and concerns
- Quality metrics and deviations

**Handoff communication:**
- Shift summary reports generated automatically
- Voice-activated documentation ("AI, note that press 3 was running hot during shift 2")

**Cross-shift coordination:**
- Issues spanning multiple shifts tracked
- Resolution ownership assigned

- Escalation paths defined

### 5. Quality Control Triage

**Defect classification:**
- Image recognition for visual defects
- Automated severity assessment
- Root cause categorization

**Issue routing:**
- "Defect batch #789 routed to quality engineering for analysis"

**Process adjustment:**
- "Defect rate increased on line 2—suggesting parameter adjustment"
