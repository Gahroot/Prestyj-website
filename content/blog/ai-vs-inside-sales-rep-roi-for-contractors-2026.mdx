You've crossed $1M in revenue. Leads are growing. But your office manager is fielding calls between dispatching techs, and your owner is calling back prospects at 9 PM. You need dedicated lead coverage. The traditional answer: hire an inside sales rep.

## The Hybrid Model: Best of Both Worlds

The most effective contractor setup in 2026 isn't "AI *or* ISR" — it's **AI for first response + qualification, ISR for closing and complex conversations**.

### How the Hybrid Works

1. **Lead comes in** → AI answers in 12–45 seconds (phone, SMS, web chat)
2. **AI qualifies** → Name, address, issue type, urgency, budget, schedule
3. **AI books appointment** → Direct into ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro / Jobber
4. **High-intent lead** → Warm transfer to ISR for real-time conversation
5. **Follow-up nurture** → AI runs multi-touch sequence over 30 days
6. **ISR focuses on** → Closing warm leads, complex quotes, relationship building

## When to Hire an ISR Instead of Deploying AI

AI isn't always the right first move. Consider an ISR if:

- **Your average ticket exceeds $15,000** — complex sales with multi-visit qualification benefit from human rapport
- **Your lead volume is under 100/month** — ISR capacity isn't the bottleneck; quality conversation is
- **You sell to commercial/Builder accounts** — long-cycle, relationship-driven sales where AI adds less value
- **You already have AI and need depth** — the hybrid model works best when you've maxed out AI's qualification ceiling

For most contractors in the $500–$5,000 ticket range with 150+ monthly leads, **AI delivers better ROI per dollar**, and the hybrid model captures the ISR advantage at a fraction of the full-time cost.

## How to Run Your Own ISR vs AI Comparison

### Step 1: Calculate Your True ISR Cost

Use the fully loaded table above. Don't just use the salary — add benefits, tools, training, management, and turnover. Most contractors find the real number is **50–65% higher** than salary.

### Step 2: Measure Your Current Response Time

Track every inbound lead for 7 days. Log the time it was received and the time a human called back. If your average exceeds 5 minutes, AI will measurably improve your close rate.

### Step 3: Model the Revenue Difference

Use this formula:

```
Revenue Difference = Monthly Leads × (AI Close Rate – Human Close Rate) × Average Ticket
```

Example: 500 leads × (18% – 12%) × $1,200 = **$36,000/month in additional revenue from AI's speed advantage alone.**

### Step 4: Factor in the ISR Hidden Costs

- Training: 4–8 weeks at 50% productivity = $3,000–$6,000 in lost output
- Turnover: 25–40% chance of replacement = $8,000–$15,000 per event
- Management: Owner spends 5–10 hours/month supervising = $750–$1,500 in opportunity cost

### Step 5: Compare TCO, Not Salary

The ISR's salary is the floor, not the ceiling. The 12-month TCO is **$94,000–$136,000**. AI's TCO is **$12,000–$60,000**. The gap is **$34,000–$104,000** — before you count the revenue advantage.
