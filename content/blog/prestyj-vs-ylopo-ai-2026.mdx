## What Is Ylopo and Raiya?

**Ylopo** is a real estate marketing platform that manages paid advertising (primarily Facebook and Google PPC) and generates leads for real estate teams and brokerages. It's a full-service platform that handles ad management, landing pages, IDX search, and lead routing.

**Raiya** is Ylopo's AI assistant — an AI-powered SMS chatbot that follows up with leads generated through the Ylopo platform. Raiya engages leads in conversation, qualifies them, and routes hot leads to agents.

Ylopo + Raiya is a comprehensive solution if you want to outsource your entire lead generation and initial follow-up to one platform.

## What Is Prestyj?

**Prestyj** is a done-for-you AI lead response and appointment setting platform built for real estate teams and brokerages. It combines:
- Done-for-you ad management (Facebook + YouTube)
- Batch video ad production (300–1,000 ads/month)
- AI lead response (sub-60-second, 24/7)
- Lead qualification and routing
- Appointment booking
- Lead reactivation
- CRM integration

Unlike Ylopo, Prestyj works with leads from any source — not just its own platform. And unlike Ylopo, Prestyj is built around done-for-you service rather than a self-managed platform.

## Key Differences

### 1. Lead Source Flexibility

**Ylopo/Raiya:** Raiya is optimized for Ylopo-generated leads. It's tightly integrated with Ylopo's lead flow, behavioral data (what properties a lead viewed), and CRM connections. If you're using Ylopo to generate all your leads, Raiya leverages that behavioral data well.

However, if you're running your own Facebook campaigns, YouTube ads, or generating leads through other channels (Zillow, Realtor.com, your IDX), Raiya doesn't extend to those sources naturally.

**Prestyj:** Works with leads from any source. Facebook ads, YouTube ads, Google PPC, Zillow, IDX, landing pages, QR codes — any lead that enters your CRM can be picked up by Prestyj's AI. There's no platform lock-in.

### 2. Done-for-You vs. Self-Managed

**Ylopo:** Ylopo manages your paid advertising for you — but it's a platform you manage the strategy within. You're responsible for knowing what's working, adjusting your lead flow, and making decisions about targeting, budget, and creative.

**Prestyj:** Fully done-for-you. Prestyj's team manages your ads, creates your video content, trains the AI, and handles the ongoing optimization. You set the goals; we handle the execution.

### 3. Video Content Production

**Ylopo:** Focuses primarily on search and Facebook ads. Limited video production.

**Prestyj:** Includes 300–1,000 batch video ads per month — short-form video content for Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. This is a significant differentiator for teams that want video-driven social advertising.

### 4. Pricing Structure

**Ylopo:** Platform fees vary based on team size and features. Most teams pay $1,000–$3,000+/month in platform fees plus their ad spend separately.

**Prestyj:** $1,997–$5,997/month all-in, including ad budget ($1,000–$2,000/month of ad spend is included). The monthly fee covers AI, ads, video production, and full management.

### 5. Platform Lock-In

**Ylopo:** If you leave Ylopo, you lose Raiya and your entire lead generation infrastructure. There's significant switching cost.

**Prestyj:** Prestyj integrates with your existing CRM and tools. If you ever switch, your CRM data, lead history, and business operations are unaffected.

## Who Should Choose Ylopo + Raiya

Ylopo is a good fit if you:
- Want a single platform to handle lead generation, IDX search, and AI follow-up
- Are comfortable with the Ylopo ecosystem and don't need leads from other sources
- Have a team of 5–50 agents who are comfortable operating within Ylopo's interface
- Want IDX search integrated with your lead nurture
- Don't need batch video ad production

Ylopo + Raiya is a solid choice for teams that are already invested in the Ylopo platform and primarily generate leads through Ylopo's campaigns.

## Who Should Choose Prestyj
