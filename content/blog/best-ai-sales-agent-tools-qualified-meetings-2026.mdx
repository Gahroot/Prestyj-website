## FAQ

**Q: Can I use an AI sales agent for inbound lead response?**
A: Most platforms focus on outbound. For inbound (answering calls, responding to web forms, replying to ads), you need a platform that handles real-time phone, text, and email response. Prestyj is one of the few that covers both inbound and outbound.

**Q: How do I evaluate which platform is right for me?**
A: Start with your primary use case: inbound lead response or outbound prospecting? Then evaluate based on: cost per qualified meeting, time to first meeting, platform management overhead, and CRM integration depth.

**Q: What's the difference between "AI sales agent" and "AI SDR"?**
A: They're used interchangeably. "AI SDR" typically refers to outbound-focused platforms that prospect and sequence. "AI sales agent" is broader — it can include inbound lead response, qualification, and booking.

**Q: How quickly will I see results?**
A: Done-for-you platforms: first meetings booked within 3–7 days. Mid-market DIY: 1–3 weeks. Enterprise: 2–4 weeks. The difference is setup and optimization time.
