## Which Provider Wins by MSP Profile

### Small MSP (1–3 outbound seats, under 1,500 dials/month)

**Pick: CallerID Reputation.** At $12–$22/line with $0 setup, it's the only option where the math pencils at low volume. Accept narrower carrier coverage as the tradeoff.

### Growing IT services firm (mixed mobile + landline, 1,500–10,000 dials/month)

**Pick: Hiya Connect or First Orion.** You're now connecting enough calls that a 27–41% answer-rate lift pays for the higher line fee several times over. Choose Hiya if your list is mostly mobile, First Orion if it's mixed.

### Multi-location MSP / scaling outbound (10,000+ dials/month)

**Pick: First Orion (ENGAGE)** for coverage, or **a bundled voice-agent provider** if you're also automating the dialing. At this volume, the operational cost of managing a separate attestation vendor often outweighs the line-fee difference.

## The ROI Math: When Branded Calling Pays for Itself

The line fee is not the real number — cost per *connected conversation* is. Worked example for a typical MSP outbound line:

| Input | Value |
|---|---|
| Branded calling line fee | $30/month |
| Outbound dials/month per line | 2,000 |
| Added cost per dial | $0.015 |
| Answer-rate lift | +27–41% |
| Fully-loaded added cost per connected call | [$0.04–$0.11](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-connected-call) |

Because you connect 27–41% more conversations on the *same* dial volume, the per-connection cost of your SDR or AI voice agent drops by roughly the same percentage. For MSP outbound — renewals, security follow-ups, win-backs — [break-even is ~800–1,500 dials/month per line](/stat/branded-calling-msp-break-even-dials). Above that, it compounds.

## Hidden Costs IT Buyers Miss

- **Per-display micro-fees** ($0.005–$0.02 each) on a small number of providers — $80–$300/month at 10k+ dials/line.
- **Attestation re-registration fees** when numbers change carriers or get re-issued (common as MSPs spin up and retire DIDs).
- **Logo/branded-name approval delays** of 5–15 business days — plan pilots around it.
- **Per-carrier coverage gaps** — display may render on AT&T but not a regional carrier. Always ask for a carrier-coverage map before signing.

---

## When Branded Calling Is NOT Worth It for an MSP

- **Inbound-only help desks.** Branded calling lifts *outbound* answer rates; inbound caller-ID reputation is a separate product.
- **Very low outbound volume** (under ~500 dials/month per line) — the break-even doesn't pencil.
- **Already running compliant local-presence dialing** that's delivering acceptable connect rates (though carriers increasingly throttle it).

**Q: What's the cheapest branded calling for a small IT services firm?**
A: CallerID Reputation at [$12–$22 per line per month](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-line) with $0 setup. The tradeoff is narrower carrier coverage than First Orion or Hiya.

**Q: What does branded calling cost per call for an MSP?**

**Q: Do I need branded calling and STIR/SHAKEN, or just one?**
A: STIR/SHAKEN is the underlying caller-ID attestation protocol; branded calling sits on top and adds the visible name and logo. You need attestation enabled to display the brand.
