## The Advertised Price vs. The Real Cost

You've seen the ads: "Live receptionists starting at $XXX/month." It sounds affordable—until you get your first bill. ## Hidden Cost #1: Setup and Onboarding Fees

Before your first call is answered, you're paying fees that rarely appear in marketing materials.

### Typical Setup Costs:

- **Account setup:** $50-150 (one-time)
- **Number porting:** $25-50
- **Script development:** $100-300
- **Agent training:** $75-200/hour
- **CRM integration:** $200-500 setup + monthly fees

**Real example:** A roofing contractor paid $497 in setup fees to AnswerConnect before taking a single call, plus $49/month for CRM integration.

## Hidden Cost #2: Premium Time Surcharges

Want coverage during the times you actually need it? That'll cost extra.

### Premium Surcharges:

| Time Period | Typical Surcharge |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Weekends** | +$0.50-1.50/minute |
| **Holidays** | +$1.00-2.50/minute |
| **After-hours** | +$0.75-1.25/minute |
| **24/7 coverage** | +40-60% to base rate |

AnswerConnect charges an additional $75-150/month for true 24/7 coverage on top of their base $289 plan. Smith.ai's "Starter" plan doesn't include weekends at all.

## Hidden Cost #3: Training and Turnover

The hidden cost of human answering services is churn. The average call center agent stays 6-12 months. Every time an agent leaves:

### Turnover Costs Per Agent:

- **Training time:** 8-16 hours of your time
- **Knowledge transfer:** 3-5 hours documenting processes
- **Ramp-up period:** 2-4 weeks of mistakes and missed calls
- **Quality issues:** 15-20% error rate during training

**Annual turnover cost:** If you have 2-3 agents covering your business, you're losing 40-60 hours per year to retraining—that's $1,200-3,000 in wasted labor.

## Hidden Cost #4: Message Delivery and Storage

Getting your messages shouldn't cost extra—but often it does.

### Common Message Fees:

- **SMS delivery:** $0.10-0.25 per message
- **Transcription fees:** $0.50-1.00 per call
- **Recording storage:** $5-15/month
- **API access:** $50-150/month
- **Custom integrations:** $200-500 setup + monthly fees

Smith.ai charges $0.20 per SMS message sent. At 150 messages per month, that's an extra $30—every single month.

## Hidden Cost #5: Volume Minimums and Commitments

Many providers require annual contracts or minimum monthly spends.

### Contract Traps:

- **Annual commitments:** 10-20% "discount" that locks you in
- **Cancellation fees:** 25-50% of remaining contract value
- **Minimum spend requirements:** "Must use at least 50 minutes monthly"
- **Price escalators:** 5-8% annual increases built into contracts

Ruby requires a 6-month minimum commitment for their "Professional" plan, with a $200 early termination fee.
