## FAQ

**Can an AI answering service really handle emergency contractor calls?**
Yes — the best trade-specific AI platforms (like Prestyj) triage emergencies conversationally, route to on-call technicians with full context, and log the job in your CRM. Generic AI platforms cannot do this reliably.

**How much does an AI answering service cost for contractors?**
Fully-loaded cost ranges from $199–$799/month for trade-specific platforms, versus $300–$1,400/month for live answering services. The AI advantage is 60–80% cost savings at equivalent or better call coverage.

**Will my customers know they're talking to AI?**
Modern AI voice agents are indistinguishable from human receptionists on short transactional calls. For contractor call handling (appointment booking, emergency triage, service questions), callers rarely notice or care.

**What about after-hours calls?**
[30–50% of contractor inbound calls come after hours](/stat/mcr-after-hours-call-share). AI answering services handle these at no premium, while live services often charge 1.5x for after-hours.
