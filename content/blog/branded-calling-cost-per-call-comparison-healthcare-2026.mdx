Healthcare practices comparing branded calling providers often focus on the per-line monthly fee — but the number that actually matters is **cost per connected conversation**. A $12/line provider with narrow carrier coverage may cost more per answer than a $35/line provider with deeper reach. This post breaks down the real cost per call across every major branded calling provider for healthcare outbound.

**TL;DR:** Branded calling cost per connected call for healthcare ranges from [$0.02–$0.11](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-connected-call) depending on provider and dial volume. At 2,000+ dials/month per line, CallerID Reputation is cheapest per line ($12–$22) but may cost more per answer due to narrower carrier coverage. First Orion ($25–$45/line) delivers the lowest cost per answer at scale because deeper carrier coverage means more displays render. Bundled voice-agent providers eliminate the separate line fee entirely.

**Direct answer:** The cheapest branded calling provider per connected call for healthcare depends on your carrier mix and dial volume. For small practices under 1,500 dials/month, CallerID Reputation has the lowest total cost. For mid-size practices at 1,500–10,000 dials/month, First Orion or Hiya delivers lower cost per answer. For high-volume operations, bundled providers win on both cost and simplicity. See the [Prestyj platform](/platform) or [book a pricing review](/book-demo) for a custom comparison.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cost per connected call ranges from [$0.02–$0.11](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-connected-call)** across providers — the per-line fee alone doesn't tell you which is cheapest.
- **Carrier coverage directly impacts cost per answer** — a provider that displays on 90% of carriers connects more conversations per dollar than one that displays on 60%.
- **At 2,000+ dials/month per line, the math favors deeper coverage** — the incremental cost of a higher per-line fee is offset by more connected conversations.
- **Bundled providers eliminate the separate line fee** — cost per minute includes branded display, simplifying the comparison.
- **Per-display micro-fees** ($0.005–$0.02 each) can add $80–$300/month at scale — ask every provider about display fees.
- **The right comparison is total campaign cost per booked appointment**, not per-line cost.

## How to Calculate Your Actual Cost Per Answer

The formula is straightforward:

```text
Cost per connected call = (monthly line fee + monthly display fees) / (total dials × answer rate × display rate)
```

Example for a mid-size healthcare practice:

| Input | CallerID Reputation | First Orion | Bundled (Prestyj) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly line fee | $18 | $35 | $0 |
| Monthly display fees | $0 | $0 | $0 |
| Total monthly cost | $18 | $35 | $0 (included in per-minute) |
| Total dials/month | 2,000 | 2,000 | 2,000 |

## When Cost Per Line Beats Cost Per Answer

**Small practices under 1,500 dials/month:** At low volume, the per-line fee dominates the math. CallerID Reputation at $12–$22/line is cheapest even if it connects fewer conversations, because the total dollar amount is small.

**Practices with mostly mobile patient lists:** If 80%+ of your outbound goes to mobile numbers, narrower carrier coverage matters less. CallerID Reputation's mobile coverage is adequate for many small practices.

---

## When Cost Per Answer Beats Cost Per Line

**Practices at 1,500–10,000 dials/month:** At mid-volume, the number of additional connected conversations from deeper coverage outweighs the per-line fee difference. First Orion or Hiya delivers more conversations per dollar.

**Multi-location health systems:** At scale, per-display micro-fees and carrier coverage gaps compound. Deep coverage prevents revenue leakage across locations.

**Practices using bundled voice agents:** If you're already paying for AI voice minutes, adding branded display through the same provider costs nothing extra per line — it's included.

## Hidden Costs in Cost-Per-Call Calculations

- **Per-display micro-fees** ($0.005–$0.02 each) — some providers charge these on top of the per-line fee. At 10,000+ dials/month, this adds $50–$200/month.
- **Attestation setup fees** ($0–$500 one-time) — amortize this over 12 months for true monthly cost.
- **Carrier coverage changes** — a provider's coverage can change as carriers update their databases. What works today may not work in 6 months.
- **Logo approval delays** — 5–15 business days for branded name/logo approval. Factor this into pilot timelines.

## FAQ

**Q: What's the cheapest branded calling per call for healthcare?**
A: At low volume (under 1,500 dials/month), CallerID Reputation at [$12–$22/line](/stat/branded-calling-cost-per-line). At mid-volume (1,500–10,000 dials/month), First Orion or Hiya delivers lower cost per answer due to deeper carrier coverage.

**Q: How do I calculate my actual cost per connected call?**
A: Divide your total monthly branded calling cost (line fee + display fees) by the number of calls that actually connect with your brand displayed. Formula: (monthly cost) / (total dials × answer rate × carrier display rate).

**Q: Do bundled providers really cost less per call?**
A: For practices already using AI voice agents, yes — branded display is included in the per-minute price with no separate line fee. For practices not using voice agents, you're comparing a per-minute cost (which includes other capabilities) to a pure line fee.

**Q: Does carrier coverage really matter that much?**
A: Yes. A provider that displays on 70% of carriers connects 30% fewer conversations than one that displays on 95% — even at the same per-line price. At 2,000 dials/month, that's 150 fewer connected conversations.
