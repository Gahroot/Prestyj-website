## TCPA Compliance: The Critical Rules

**TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) violations cost $500-1,500 per text.** Non-compliance can destroy your business. AI must follow strict rules:

### 1. Consent Is Mandatory

You MUST have written consent before texting. Acceptable consent methods:

‚úÖ **Checkbox on web form:** "I consent to receive text messages from [Company]"
‚úÖ **SMS opt-in:** "Text START to 12345 to receive updates"
‚úÖ **Verbal + documented:** Call recording where they agree to texts

‚ùå **NOT acceptable:** "By submitting this form you agree to our terms" (buried consent)
‚ùå **NOT acceptable:** Adding phone numbers from business cards to SMS list

### 2. Identify Yourself Immediately

First message must include:
- Your name or company name
- Purpose of message
- Opt-out instructions

**Compliant first message:**
"Hi! This is Sarah from Prestyj. You requested info about homes in Austin. Reply STOP to opt out."

### 3. Honor Opt-Outs Instantly

When someone replies STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, or similar:
- Remove from list immediately (within 1 business hour)
- Send confirmation: "You've been unsubscribed. Reply START to rejoin."
- Never text again unless they re-opt-in

### 4. Time Restrictions

Do not text:
- Before 8am or after 9pm in recipient's time zone
- This means AI must handle time zones automatically

### 5. Keep Records

Document:
- When and how consent was obtained
- All opt-ins and opt-outs
- Message content and timestamps

**AI Advantage:** Automated compliance. AI never forgets to check consent, never texts at 11pm, auto-processes STOP commands.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Texting Without Consent

**The error:** Adding leads to SMS without explicit opt-in

**The consequence:** $500-1,500 per message TCPA fine

**The fix:** Only text leads who checked the consent box or replied to an initial opt-in message

### Mistake 2: Generic, Robotic Messages

**The error:** "Dear Customer, we have a special offer for you"

**The consequence:** 0% response rate, feels like spam

**The fix:** Personalize to lead source, behavior, and stated interests. Use conversational language.

### Mistake 3: Over-Texting

**The error:** Sending 3+ messages per day without responses

**The consequence:** Spam reports, opt-outs, brand damage

**The fix:** Respect response cadence. If they're not replying, slow down or try a different channel.

### Mistake 4: No Human Backup

**The error:** AI handles everything, including complex questions it can't answer

**The consequence:** Leads get frustrated, drop out

**The fix:** AI should recognize when to escalate. "That's a great question‚Äîlet me connect you with [Name] who specializes in that."

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Time Zones

**The error:** Texting at 10pm local time because it's 7pm where you are

**The consequence:** TCPA violations, angry prospects

**The fix:** AI must auto-detect time zones and respect 8am-9pm windows

### What's the average response time with AI SMS?

AI responds in 8-60 seconds to every text, 24/7. Compare to average human response time of 2+ hours (often 48+ hours evenings/weekends).
