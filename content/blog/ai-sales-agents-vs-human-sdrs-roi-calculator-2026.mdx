## Step3: Calculate Projected Returns

### For Each Scenario:

```
AI meetings booked = (Inbound + Outbound) × AI response rate × AI contact rate × AI booking rate
Human meetings booked = (Inbound + Outbound) × Human response rate × Human contact rate × Human booking rate
AI cost per meeting = AI monthly cost / AI meetings booked
Human cost per meeting = Human monthly cost / Human meetings booked
```

### 12-Month ROI:

```
AI 12-month ROI = (AI meetings × close rate × deal size - AI 12-month cost) / AI 12-month cost
Human 12-month ROI = (Human meetings × close rate × deal size - Human 12-month cost) / Human 12-month cost
```

## Adjusting for Your Specific Factors

### Higher AI ROI If:
- High inbound lead volume (200+ leads/month)
- Short sales cycle (close within 30 days)
- Multiple products/services to cross-sell
- After-hours lead capture is critical

### Lower AI ROI If:
- Very low lead volume (under 50/month)
- Complex enterprise sales (6+ month cycle)
- High-ACV deals ($100K+) requiring human relationship building
- Regulated industry requiring human oversight on all communications

## FAQ

**Q: How accurate is this calculator?**
A: The calculator provides benchmarks based on data from thousands of AI sales agent deployments. Your actual results will depend on lead quality, sales cycle, and offer. Most companies see results within 20–30% of the base case.

**Q: What's the minimum lead volume for AI to make sense?**
A: Any company processing 50+ leads/month can benefit from AI. The ROI increases significantly at 200+ leads/month where human SDRs can't keep up.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: Most companies see initial results within 2–4 weeks of deployment. Full ROI typically takes 60–90 days to materialize.

**Q: Should I replace human SDRs entirely with AI?**
A: For most companies, the best model is hybrid: AI for speed, volume, and qualification; humans for complex discovery, relationship building, and high-value deals.
