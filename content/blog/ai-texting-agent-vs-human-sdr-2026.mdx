## When AI Texting Wins

- **Inbound lead response from paid ads:** Facebook, Google, and lead aggregator leads need instant response or they go cold.
- **After-hours lead engagement:** [30–50% of leads](/stat/mcr-after-hours-call-share) submit forms or call outside business hours.
- **Database reactivation:** Working through 10,000+ dormant contacts is impossible for humans; AI does it in days.
- **High-volume lead sources:** When you're generating 100+ leads/day, human SDRs can't keep up.
- **Consistent qualification:** AI asks every qualifying question, every time. No drift, no skipped steps.

---

## When Human SDRs Still Win

- **Complex consultative sales:** When qualification requires deep product knowledge and strategic thinking.
- **Relationship-driven deals:** When the prospect needs to trust the person on the other end.
- **High-value accounts:** When the deal size justifies human attention ($50K+ ACV).
- **Emotional nuance:** When the prospect is frustrated, confused, or needs empathy.

## The Hybrid Model

Most successful teams use a hybrid model:

1. **AI handles first contact** (under 60 seconds) and initial qualification via SMS
2. **AI books the appointment** or routes to a human for complex deals
3. **Humans handle** demos, negotiations, and relationship management

This gives you AI speed at the top of the funnel and human expertise at the bottom — at 30–50% of the cost of a human-only team.

## FAQ

**How fast can an AI texting agent respond?**
12–45 seconds for inbound leads. The best platforms respond faster than a human can read a lead notification.

**Is AI texting compliant?**
Yes — managed platforms handle TCPA, 10DLC registration, consent tracking, and opt-out management. Verify compliance features before signing.

**Do leads know they're texting with AI?**
Most don't notice or care — conversational AI is indistinguishable from human texting for qualification conversations. The best platforms disclose AI identity when asked.

**Can AI texting replace my ISA team?**
For most service businesses (under 50 leads/day), yes. For high-volume operators (200+ leads/day), AI is the first layer with humans handling escalations.
