## The Three Categories of Batch Video Ad Services

### 1. Managed / Done-For-You (Best for Results)

**Prestyj**

Full-service batch video ad production. One 15–20 minute recording session turns into 300–1,000 ad variations. Includes creative direction, script optimization, platform-specific formatting, and weekly delivery. The only category that includes managed ad spend and AI agents at higher tiers.

**When to choose:** You want winning ads, not a video production project. You don't have an in-house editor or creative director. You want creative testing at scale.

**Cost profile:** Higher monthly cost but lowest cost per winning ad because volume + managed optimization produces more winners per dollar.

### 2. DIY AI Avatar Tools (Best for Budget Control)

**Arcads, HeyGen, Creatify**

AI-powered platforms that generate UGC-style video ads using AI avatars. You write scripts, select avatars, and render videos. Low per-video cost but requires creative direction, editing, and ad management expertise.

**When to choose:** You have a media buyer or creative director who can script and optimize ads. You want maximum control over creative.

**Cost profile:** Lowest per-video cost. But creative direction time (10–20 hours/month at $50–$100/hour) and the lower winner rate for AI avatar ads (vs real-person recordings) pushes cost per winning ad above managed services.

### 3. UGC Creator Marketplaces (Best for Authentic UGC)

**Billo, Insense**

Marketplaces that connect you with UGC creators who film custom ad content. Higher quality and authenticity than AI avatars but low volume and slow turnaround.

**When to choose:** You want authentic UGC creator content and can wait 2–4 weeks for delivery. You need 5–30 videos, not 300.

**Cost profile:** Highest cost per video ($50–$200). Best for supplementing batch production with hero creative, not for volume testing.

## FAQ

**What's the cheapest way to produce video ads at scale?**
DIY AI avatar tools (HeyGen, Arcads) are cheapest per video ($2–$10). But when you factor in creative direction time and the lower winner rate for AI-generated ads, managed services (Prestyj) produce winning ads at lower total cost.

**How many video ads do I need per month?**
For meaningful creative testing on Meta, [300+ per month](/blog/batch-video-ads-complete-guide-2026) is the benchmark. Below 100, you don't have enough volume to identify winning patterns.

**What's a good cost per winning ad?**
$300–$800 per winning ad is achievable with batch video production. Traditional production yields winners at $2,000–$8,000+. The difference is volume: batch produces more ads, so even at a lower winner rate, you get more winners per dollar.

**Should I use AI avatars or real recordings?**
Real recordings (like Prestyj's approach) outperform AI avatars on conversion rates for most service business and real estate ads. AI avatars work for B2B and corporate messaging. Use AI avatars for volume supplementation, real recordings for primary creative.
