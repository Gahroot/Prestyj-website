## FAQs

### What HVAC ad ideas should I test first?

Start with replacement urgency, repair warning signs, maintenance plans, financing, and trust. Those map to real HVAC buying triggers.

### Should HVAC ads feature the owner?

Often yes. Owner-led creative builds trust faster than generic stock footage or actors, especially for high-ticket replacement decisions.

### How many HVAC ads should I make?

Start with 100 for one offer or 300 to 500 if you need to test repair, replacement, maintenance, and financing separately.
