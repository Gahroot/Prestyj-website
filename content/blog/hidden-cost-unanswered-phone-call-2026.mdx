## FAQ

**Q: How do I know how many calls I'm actually missing?**
A: Pull your call logs for the last 30 days. Count calls that went to voicemail, calls that rang more than 3 times before answer, and calls that came in outside business hours. Most businesses are shocked — the number is usually 2–3x what they expected.

**Q: Won't an AI receptionist frustrate callers who want a "real person"?**
A: Modern AI voice agents handle natural conversation, understand context, and book appointments in real time. Callers typically can't tell the difference. The ones who *can* tell are the 2–5% of high-emotion calls that should route to a human — and that's configurable.

**Q: Is this just an after-hours problem?**
A: No. The biggest losses happen *during* business hours when staff are on jobs, in meetings, or helping walk-in customers. A 22% miss rate during business hours at a busy plumbing company is typical.

**Q: What's the fastest way to measure my missed-call cost?**
A: Multiply your monthly inbound calls × your missed-call percentage × your average revenue per booked job. If you don't track missed calls, check your phone system's call logs or ask your answering service for a report.
