## Where AI Converts Better for Home Services

### 1. Missed Call Text-Back
AI texts within 60 seconds of a missed call: "Hey, saw you just called — what can we help with?" This recovers 15–25% of missed calls.

### 2. After-Hours Emergency Capture
AI qualifies the emergency, collects details, and either dispatches or schedules the first available appointment — 24/7.

### 3. Estimate Follow-Up
AI follows up on estimates 24–72 hours later, answers questions, and books the job — recovering 15–25% of cold estimates.

### 4. Seasonal Promotion Outreach
AI can call/text past customers about seasonal tune-ups, maintenance plans, and promotions — generating recurring revenue without manual effort.

## Where Human SDRs Still Win

### 1. High-Ticket Estimates
Roofing, HVAC replacement, and remodeling estimates over $10,000 benefit from human relationship building and detailed consultation.

### 2. Complex Jobs
Multi-trade jobs, commercial projects, and insurance claim work require human judgment and coordination.

### 3. Relationship Selling
Past customer relationships, referral partnerships, and commercial accounts benefit from personal touch.
