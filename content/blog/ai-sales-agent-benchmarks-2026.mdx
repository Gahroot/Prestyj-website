## FAQ

**Q: What's a "good" lead-to-booking rate for an AI sales agent?**
A: 25–40% for inbound leads, 5–12% for outbound. If your AI agent is below 15% for inbound, something is misconfigured.

**Q: How long does it take to hit benchmark performance?**
A: Top performers hit benchmark within 2–4 weeks. Median performance typically takes 4–8 weeks. If you're not at median by week 8, your platform or configuration needs attention.

**Q: Can AI sales agents really outperform human SDRs on conversion rate?**
A: Yes — primarily because AI responds faster (60 seconds vs 42 hours), follows up consistently (100% vs 40–60%), and operates 24/7. The conversion rate advantage comes from speed and consistency, not persuasion.

**Q: What should I do if my AI agent is underperforming?**
A: Check: (1) response time — is it under 60 seconds? (2) follow-up — is every lead getting 3+ touches? (3) booking flow — is the calendar link working and available? (4) qualification — is the AI asking the right questions? (5) lead quality — are your ads generating qualified leads?
