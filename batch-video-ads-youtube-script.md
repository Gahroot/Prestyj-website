The problem might be much simpler.

You might be spending money before you actually know which message your customers care about.

And that sounds obvious when I say it out loud, but this is the part almost every business gets wrong.

We run one ad.

Maybe three ads.

Maybe we test a couple different videos.

And sometimes those things are true.

But a lot of the time, the real issue is that we never tested enough messages to know what was actually wrong.

We didn’t test enough hooks.

We didn’t test enough pain points.

We didn’t test enough offers.

We didn’t test enough angles.

We basically walked into a market, said one or two things, got ignored, and then assumed the whole market didn’t want what we sell.
