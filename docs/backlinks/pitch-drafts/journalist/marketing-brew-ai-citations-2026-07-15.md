**Subject:** Original Bing AI data: citations rose 86% as buyer-math pages won

Hi Marketing Brew team,

We analyzed two consecutive Bing Webmaster AI Search exports for Prestyj and found a pattern that could fit your AI-search coverage: citation growth clustered around the numbers marketers need to make a buying decision—pricing, ROI, response times, and hidden costs—rather than broad AI explainers.

From June 18 to June 29, 2026:

- Page citations rose **6,724 → 12,514 (+86%)**.
- Query citations rose **4,799 → 9,584 (+100%)**.
- Distinct cited pages rose **79 → 107 (+35%)**.
- The top query asked how to evaluate YouTube media-testing pricing (**358 citations**).
- Other leading queries focused on hidden AI voice-agent costs, answering-service comparisons, database-reactivation ROI, and AI sales-agent pricing.

The useful story is not “AI likes listicles.” In this single-site case study, the citation winners front-loaded hard numbers, comparison tables, and the costs vendors tend to omit. That is a concrete editorial pattern marketers can test—and a warning that generic AI explainers may create volume without citation visibility.
