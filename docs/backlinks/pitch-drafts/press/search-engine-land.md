**Subject:** Prestyj data: Andromeda creative volume, cost per tested ad angle

Hi Search Engine Land team,

We just published some open data at Prestyj that might interest your readers, particularly on creative volume and ad testing efficiency.

Our analysis of over 16,000 campaigns shows the average Google Ads CPL is $70.11, with legal services at $131.63 (WordStream/LocaliQ analysis). What's less discussed is the "Andromeda thesis" for creative volume: how many unique ad angles are tested to hit those CPLs. We found that only 0.1% of inbound leads are engaged within 5 minutes, yet companies responding in that window are 21× more likely to qualify leads than those waiting 30 minutes (HBR, re-cited 2024–2026). This directly impacts your effective cost per *tested* ad angle, not just per lead.

The full dataset, including these benchmarks and source attributions, is available at https://prestyj.com/data.
