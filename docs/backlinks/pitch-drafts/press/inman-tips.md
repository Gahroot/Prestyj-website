Hi Inman team,

Sending this to tips@ as a data story, not a pitch — there's a stat in here I think your agent readership would argue about in the comments, which is usually a good sign.

We just published an open dataset on lead response, and the headline finding is uncomfortable for anyone running a paid-lead pipeline:

- **51% of leads are never contacted at all** (InsideSales).
- **57% of companies take a full week or more** to respond to an inbound lead.
- Meanwhile **over 50% of consumers hire the first business that responds** — even if it costs more (Forbes).
- The math behind it: a 5-minute reply is **21× more likely to qualify** the lead than a 30-minute one (Harvard Business Review).
