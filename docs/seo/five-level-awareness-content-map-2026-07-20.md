## Awareness definitions

1. **Unaware:** The buyer sees a business symptom but has not named the problem.
2. **Problem aware:** The buyer understands the leak and wants its cost or cause.
3. **Solution aware:** The buyer compares categories or operating models.
4. **Product aware:** The buyer evaluates providers, software, or implementation fit.
5. **Most aware:** The buyer checks price, pilot terms, timeline, and break-even economics.
