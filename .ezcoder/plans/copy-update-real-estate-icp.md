# Copy Update Plan: Real Estate ICP

## ICP Definition
**Real estate team leaders and brokerage owners** doing $20M+ in annual volume, running $1K+/month in paid ads, who think like business owners — not agents working a job.

**Core insight**: They're already spending money to generate leads. Their pain is *conversion*, not acquisition. Every lead they pay for that goes unanswered is pure money on the floor.

**Differentiator**: Prestyj isn't a bot — it's a dedicated AI team member that replies as a natural extension of your team. "Hey, it's Jamie from [Brokerage Name]..." That framing makes it feel white-glove, not robotic.

### 9. FAQ Data — `src/lib/faq-data.ts`

**Replace all 6 FAQs with real estate team leader focused versions:**

1. **Q: Will my leads know they're talking to AI?**
   > A: No — and that's intentional. Your AI team member replies as a named person on your team ("Hey, it's Alex from [Your Brokerage]"). The conversation is natural, contextual, and on-brand. Leads engage just as well as with a human assistant — often better, because they hear back in under 60 seconds.

2. **Q: How is this different from hiring an ISA?**
   > A: A good ISA costs $4,000–$6,000/month, works 9-to-5, takes vacations, and eventually quits. Your AI team member works 24/7, never calls in sick, doesn't need HR management, and costs a fraction of that. It handles the front-end qualification so your ISA — if you keep one — only touches the hot leads.

3. **Q: Does it work with our CRM?**
   > A: Yes. Prestyj integrates with Follow Up Boss, Sierra Interactive, kvCORE, HubSpot, Salesforce, and more. Leads flow in, get worked, and qualified contacts sync back to your CRM with full conversation history. Setup is typically under 30 minutes.

4. **Q: What kind of leads does it handle?**
   > A: Buyers, sellers, investors — any inbound lead from your paid ads, website, or organic sources. It also re-engages old leads sitting in your database. If you've got a list of leads you've given up on, your AI team member can work those too.

5. **Q: What happens when a lead asks something it can't answer?**
   > A: It hands off cleanly. You get notified immediately with full conversation context so you can jump in without the lead ever feeling dropped. It's designed to handle qualification, not replace your expertise on the complex stuff.

6. **Q: Is there a long-term contract?**
   > A: No. Month-to-month. We earn your business because the results speak — most team leaders see ROI in the first week. We don't need contracts to keep clients.
